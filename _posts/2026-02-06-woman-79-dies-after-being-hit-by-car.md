A 79-year-old woman tragically passed away after being hit by a car in Prestwich, Greater Manchester, on Thursday. The incident occurred on Bury New Road near Butterstile Lane and Scholes Lane. Emergency services responded around 21:00 GMT, and the area was cordoned off for investigation. A man in his 40s was arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving.
