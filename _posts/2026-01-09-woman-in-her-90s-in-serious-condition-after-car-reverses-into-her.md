A woman in her 90s is in a serious condition after being hit by a reversing car in Failsworth, north Manchester, on January 7, 2026. The incident occurred around 2pm on Albert Road West. Greater Manchester Police are urging anyone with information or dashcam footage related to the collision to step forward. They specifically want witnesses who saw the vehicle beforehand or the collision itself. To report, you can call the Serious Collision Investigation Unit at 0161 856 4741, use their Live Chat on gmp.police.uk with the log number 1936 of 07/01/2026, or reach out to Crimestoppers anonymously at 0800 555 111.
