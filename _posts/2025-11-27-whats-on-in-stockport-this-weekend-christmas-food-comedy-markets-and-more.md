Stockport is buzzing with events this weekend! Sponsored by EatGoodWest UK, Worker Bee Markets, and the Waterside Hotel & Leisure Club, there's something for everyone. Check out the festive lunches at the Waterside Hotel from 27 November to 19 December, with a three-course Christmas menu for £34.95. On 14 December, enjoy a Sunday Carvery for £16.95, with unlimited veggies and gravy.

For a laugh, don’t miss "A Laugh in Stockport" comedy night at Thread on 27 November featuring Peter Brush and others for £13.20. If you're into handcrafted goodies, the Worker Bee festive pop-up market at Morley Nurseries on 29 November will have homemade produce and unique gifts.

Also, from 30 November to 19 December, Goofus Theatre will take over local museums with free family-friendly shows. Make sure to book your tickets in advance! For more local happenings, check the What's On section or submit your own event. Enjoy your weekend in Stockport!
