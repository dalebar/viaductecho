A mother from Bolton is heartbroken after losing her first-born son in a tragic car crash. She expressed the profound emptiness she feels, describing him as "my heart, my soul, my everything," and noted that he taught her how to be a mother. He brought joy to everyone around him and will always be deeply loved. His sister shared her grief, saying she can't imagine living without him.
