Emergency services are responding to an incident outside Holy Trinity Primary School in Bury, which has resulted in Cecil Street being cordoned off. Parents were notified that the school will remain closed today, causing concern among families. The nature of the incident is still unclear, but police were present in the area earlier in the week. A local mother expressed her worries after receiving conflicting messages about her children's attendance. Forensic investigators are on-site collecting evidence, and updates from Greater Manchester Police are pending. The situation is still developing, and the community is watching closely for more information.
