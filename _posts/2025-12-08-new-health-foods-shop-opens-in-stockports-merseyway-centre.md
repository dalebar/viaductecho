Grape Tree, a well-known health food retailer, has just opened a new shop in the Merseyway Shopping Centre, right next to Barrhead Travel. The store features a wide selection of organic and vegan health foods, including supplements, fruits, nuts, seeds, and vitamins. It’s open daily, with hours from 9am to 5.30pm Monday to Saturday and 10.30am to 4.30pm on Sundays. This Stockport store is part of Grape Tree’s larger chain, which has 177 branches nationwide. They promise a variety of health foods and wellbeing products, including their own 100% Organic range and healthy snacks. You can also find personal care items and supplements.
