Police are on the lookout for info regarding a bank card theft outside Morrisons in Cheadle Heath at about 5.25pm on 19 November 2025. The stolen card was later used at various spots, including Sainsbury's. They’re keen to identify two men in the pictures who might help with the investigation. If you’ve got any details, don’t hesitate to reach out to Greater Manchester Police on 101, quoting log 2834 from 19/11/2025.
