A woman from Manchester, Cherry, received a desperate plea for help from a mum trapped in a flat fire in Hong Kong with her newborn. Cherry was deeply affected and couldn't sleep that night. She suspects that ongoing maintenance, flammable materials, and faulty fire alarms contributed to the disaster. Eight individuals have been arrested on suspicion of corruption related to the building's renovation work.
