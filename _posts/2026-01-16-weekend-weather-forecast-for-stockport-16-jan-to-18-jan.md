This weekend in Stockport, expect a mix of cloudy skies and single-digit temperatures. Friday kicks off with partly cloudy weather, peaking at 7°C and dropping to 2°C overnight—perfect for staying in! Saturday will see a higher chance of rain at 58%, with temps reaching 8°C during the day, so grab an umbrella if you're heading out. Sunday brings more clouds and a high of 7°C, with humidity peaking at 93%, but winds will be calmer. 

If you're commuting, Friday's the best day weather-wise, while Saturday and Sunday might bring some rain—so be cautious on the roads. For plans, Friday is ideal for outdoor activities. On Saturday, consider indoor options or stay flexible, while Sunday is a good day for those not fussed about a bit of grey weather. Remember to wrap up warm, it’ll be a chilly one!
