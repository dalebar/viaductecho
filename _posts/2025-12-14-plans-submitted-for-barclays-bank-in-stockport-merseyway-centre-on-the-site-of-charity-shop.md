Plans have been put forward for a new Barclays banking hall in the Merseyway shopping centre, Stockport. The proposal involves transforming the YMCA shop on Arden Walk, complete with a modern interior, new signage, and a revamped shopfront. The bank aims to operate Monday to Saturday, from 9am to 5:30pm, and will feature an ATM outside, alongside various facilities like consulting offices and a waiting area inside. It will employ about eight full-time and five part-time staff. The timeline for opening hinges on planning approval, which Stockport Council is set to decide by 27 January 2026.
