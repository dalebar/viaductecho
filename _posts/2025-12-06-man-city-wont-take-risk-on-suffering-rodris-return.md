Pep Guardiola has shared that Manchester City's Rodri is currently "suffering" and "struggling" as he tries to shake off hamstring issues. This comes after a tough spell for Rodri, who missed nearly all of last season due to a serious knee injury from a match against Arsenal in September 2024. Despite this setback, he managed to claim the Ballon d'Or shortly after his ACL injury, recognising him as the best footballer of the year, having only lost one game for club and country and helping Spain win the 2024 European Championship.
