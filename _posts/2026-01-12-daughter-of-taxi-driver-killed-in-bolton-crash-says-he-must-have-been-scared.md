A taxi driver's daughter, shaken by her father's death in a head-on crash that also claimed three teenagers' lives, expressed her disbelief, saying it all feels like "a bad dream." The collision left several others injured, with a 29-year-old woman facing life-threatening injuries. Det Insp Andrew Page mentioned that the investigation is still underway to determine what exactly happened. Humayra Ali described the situation as "absolutely devastating" and revealed that the family is struggling to cope with the loss.
