Laurence Moore, a veteran from Marple in Stockport, is urging locals to support the armed forces community this Christmas. He highlights how the festive season can be particularly tough for veterans, especially those facing physical or mental challenges. Residents can share messages of hope, either in-person or through the Help for Heroes message wall online. Last year saw thousands of supportive messages, and Laurence hopes Greater Manchester will step up again. After struggling with PTSD, he shared how a simple Christmas card from Help for Heroes made him feel connected, reminding him he wasn't alone.
