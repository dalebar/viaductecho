This weekend in Stockport is packed with events! Thanks to sponsors EatGoodWest UK and Waterside Hotel & Leisure Club, here's a quick rundown. 

- **Honda Dreamy Sales Event** at Swansway Honda (until Sun 22 February): Big savings on new models, including £2,000 off the e:Ny1 electric vehicle. 

- **Murder Inc presents: Decomposed** at Thread, Market Place (Thu 19 February): Join a hilarious murder mystery show where you suggest the characters. 

- **Flecky Bennett's Original Stockport Ghost Walk** (Fri 20 February, 7pm): Experience ghostly tales as Flecky leads you through the oldest parts of Stockport, starting at the Plaza Theatre.

- **Meet Nick Sharratt** at Simply Books, Bramhall (Sat 21 February, 3.30-4.30pm): The beloved illustrator will be signing copies of his new picture book, “Mullets.”

For more details or to submit your own event, head to the dedicated "What's On" section. Don't miss out on the fun!
