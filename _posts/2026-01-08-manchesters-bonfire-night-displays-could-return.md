Manchester might be able to host public events like bonfires and firework displays again, thanks to the government's increased funding for the next financial year. However, officials are clear that they'll approach spending the extra funds thoughtfully. The government’s reforms to local finance were a key aim for Labour, with councils receiving a £20m "recovery grant" in the first year, and plans for the long haul have been outlined.
