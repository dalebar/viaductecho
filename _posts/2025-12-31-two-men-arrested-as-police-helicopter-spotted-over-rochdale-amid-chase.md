Two men, aged 28 and 38, were arrested in Rochdale after a police chase on December 30. Eyewitnesses reported the pursuit through a local estate, which ended dramatically on Milkstone Road around 1:30 PM, with several police vehicles on the scene and a helicopter overhead. Both men were taken into custody on suspicion of dangerous driving, but no further details have been shared yet.
