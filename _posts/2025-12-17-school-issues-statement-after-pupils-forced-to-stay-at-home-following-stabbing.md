A school in Bury had to close after a stabbing incident late on Tuesday night. Four individuals were arrested, including two 23-year-old men and two 16-year-old boys, following the attack that injured a 19-year-old man. Greater Manchester Police cordoned off Cecil Street for investigation purposes, impacting both Holy Trinity Primary School and St Marie's RC Primary. Holy Trinity was initially closed as a precaution, but later stayed shut for the rest of the day, though the school assured parents that no pupils or staff were at risk.
