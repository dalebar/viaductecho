However, fact-checking revealed that the supposed quote isnâ€™t found in the actual article, nor anywhere else online. A reverse image search showed that while the image of Goodwin was from a genuine interview, the quote was likely fabricated. Buckley admitted he had seen the post multiple times, leading him to believe it was real, but stated that he would remove it if proven false, stressing the importance of verifying information during the fast-paced election period. The Manchester Evening News reached out to Reform UK for a statement.
