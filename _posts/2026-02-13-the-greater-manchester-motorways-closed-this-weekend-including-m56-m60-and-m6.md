Heads up, everyone! This weekend, several motorways in the Greater Manchester area are set for closures. Drivers are advised to plan ahead to avoid potential delays. The closures include the M56, M60, M62, M67, and A627M, mainly starting around 9pm or 11pm and lifting by 5am or 6am, which could impact early birds or night owls.

A few key closures to note: 
- M56 Westbound closing between Junctions 4-5 and 5 exit slip, both from 9pm to 5am. 
- M60 Clockwise, various junction closures, including 6 and 8, until 6am.
- M62 Westbound closures at several junctions starting at 9pm.
