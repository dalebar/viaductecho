Macclesfield FC is heartbroken after the untimely death of 21-year-old player Ethan McLeod, who tragically died in a car crash near Milton Keynes following a match on December 16. Despite attempts to save him, he was pronounced dead at the scene. The club described him as a talented, respected player with a vibrant personality who inspired those around him. In tribute to Ethan, Macclesfield FC has opened a book of condolence for fans to share their respects, which will be sent to his family. The club expressed deep sorrow, highlighting that words can't capture the loss felt by the entire community. The motorway was closed for several hours as authorities investigated the incident, calling for witnesses or dash-cam footage to come forward. Ethan's legacy will undoubtedly live on in the hearts of his teammates, coaching staff, and supporters.
