Church Street West in Radcliffe was closed this morning following a two-vehicle crash just before 11am, where one car ended up flipped on its roof. Bury Council put a diversion route in place and reminded drivers to allow extra travel time. Thankfully, no injuries have been reported. The road was cordoned off but reopened by 11:45am, with access to Radcliffe Medical Practice remaining unaffected.
