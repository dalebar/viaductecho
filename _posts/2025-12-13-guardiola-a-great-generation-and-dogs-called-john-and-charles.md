The Football Interview is a fresh series with host Kelly Somers chatting with top names in sport and entertainment about football. In her conversation with Bernardo Silva, who’s played the most games under Pep Guardiola, they dive into various topics. Kelly asks why he chose football, who influenced his career, and if there's one game he'd love to relive. She touches on the challenges he's faced at Manchester City and reflects on his childhood. Kelly even gets into his ideal day off. It’s all about getting to know the man behind the player!
