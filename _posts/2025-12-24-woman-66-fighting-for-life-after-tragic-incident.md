A 66-year-old woman is in critical condition following a crash in Bolton where her Jaguar XF collided with a parked VW Tiguan on Wigan Road. The incident occurred on December 23, and initial reports suggest she might have suffered a medical episode before the crash. The man in the parked car, aged 49, sustained minor injuries and was taken to hospital for precautionary measures. Greater Manchester Police are investigating and have appealed for witnesses or any footage of the incident. Detective Constable Ian Lawton expressed concern for the woman's family, especially with Christmas approaching, highlighting the tragic nature of the event. Anyone with information is encouraged to reach out to GMP.
