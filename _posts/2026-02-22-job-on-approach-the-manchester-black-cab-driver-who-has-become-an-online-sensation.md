Mike Daniels, a Mancunian taxi driver with 38 years of experience, has turned into an internet sensation through his Instagram account, @mileswithmike, where he shares heartwarming and comical moments from his daily journeys in Manchester. At 62, Mike delights his nearly 70,000 followers with stories ranging from navigating difficult passengers to glimpses of his lunch packed by his elusive wife, Mrs D. His recent popular posts include interactions with police and touching moments, like helping someone with dementia. Mike returned to driving after a break, appreciating the city's transformation over 18 years away. He also offers bespoke tours of Manchester, sharing its rich history and engaging with both locals and tourists. Despite the challenges of competition from apps like Uber, Mike loves what he does, finding joy in the diverse stories his passengers share and the connections he makes. Always upbeat, he considers his cab a "confessional," enabling a unique bond with people.
