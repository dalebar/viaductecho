Lewis Rimmer, a 41-year-old dad from Bromley Cross, is recovering in hospital after being critically injured when a Range Rover struck him outside Sainsbury's on October 27. He had stopped by for a lottery ticket while celebrating his daughter's birthday. The driver, a 56-year-old man, was arrested on suspicion of dangerous driving and remains on bail. Lewis has suffered serious injuries but is reportedly getting stronger, as his partner Sarah shares hopeful updates. Close friend Andy Tonge noted the incident has resonated with many, especially as it took place in a local spot frequented by families. Greater Manchester Police are appealing for witnesses or anyone with video footage of the event to come forward.
