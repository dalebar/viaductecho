Molly, a beloved legend known for her dancing and karaoke at The Chestergate pub, has sadly passed away at 86. Celebrated for her warmth, humour, and musical spirit, she was a cherished figure in the community. Her family shared the news with heavy hearts, revealing that Molly’s passing was sudden and that she had hoped to make it until Christmas. Her family expressed gratitude for the love she received from everyone, stating she’s now at peace with her late husband, Bri. To honour her memory, they’ve started a fundraiser for her send-off, with any extra funds going to St Ann’s Hospice and The Christie.
