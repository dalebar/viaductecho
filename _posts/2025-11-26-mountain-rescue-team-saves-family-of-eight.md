A family of eight got stuck on a tricky mountain path and needed rescuing. Greater Manchester Police arrived first but couldn't get them down safely, so they called in the Oldham Mountain Rescue Team. Thankfully, the family was dressed suitably for the outdoors, though they were feeling the cold while waiting for help.
