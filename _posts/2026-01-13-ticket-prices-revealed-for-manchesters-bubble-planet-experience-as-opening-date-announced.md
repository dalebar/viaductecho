Tickets are now on sale for the Bubble Planet Experience, launching in February at Depot Mayfield in Manchester. This immersive adventure invites visitors into a dreamy world where bubbles take the spotlight, featuring vibrant colours, lights, and textures. After successful runs in cities like New York and London, the experience is co-produced by Exhibition Hub and Fever. Visitors can explore 10 themed rooms, including highlights like The Bubble Ocean and The Infinity Room, designed to engage all ages with playful discovery. Ticket prices start at £17.90 for kids, £23.90 for teens, with discounts available for students and seniors. VIP options are also on offer, adding fun extras like a VR experience. Children under two go free, making it a family-friendly outing. Hamza El Azhar, the CEO of Exhibition Hub, expressed excitement about bringing this delightful experience to Manchester’s vibrant culture. Tickets can be purchased online.
