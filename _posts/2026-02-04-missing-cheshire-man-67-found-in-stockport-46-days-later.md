A 67-year-old man, missing since December 19 after leaving Leighton Hospital in Crewe, has been found safe and well 46 days later. Greater Manchester Police located him in Stockport on February 3. A spokesperson from Cheshire Police confirmed the good news, letting everyone know that he was indeed found in good health.
