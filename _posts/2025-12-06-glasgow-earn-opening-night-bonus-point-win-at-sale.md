Glasgow Warriors kicked off their Champions Cup campaign with a brilliant bonus-point win over Sale Sharks. Sale took an early lead thanks to skipper Van Rhyn, but then had to shift things around after Rekeiti Ma'asi-White got injured. Glasgow's Rory Darge shared their excitement on BBC Radio 5 Live. Next up, Sale will need to bounce back against Clermont next Saturday, while Glasgow is set to host the six-time champs, Toulouse.
