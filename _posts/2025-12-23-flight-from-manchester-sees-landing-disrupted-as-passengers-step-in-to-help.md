A Norwegian flight from Manchester to Copenhagen faced a disruption during descent due to an incident involving a child. The flight, D83523, which took off on December 22, was meant to land at 11.35am but had to pull up unexpectedly. Passengers reported chaos on board as crew and fellow passengers stepped in to help. The aircraft circled before safely landing at 12.15pm. Norwegian confirmed the situation was unforeseen and sensitive, appreciating the crew and passengers' efforts but refrained from providing further details.
