There’s been some backlash regarding Michael Carrick’s team selections at Manchester United, which is a big deal for any new manager. Critics are sharing their thoughts, often with the benefit of hindsight and without fully grasping what each staff member brings to the table. Carrick’s coaching team includes Jonny Evans, a player who thrived under the author at West Brom. They mention how managing now is leagues apart from the past, influencing coaching dynamics too. Reflecting on their own career, they've worked with notable figures like Lindsay Parsons and Dave Kemp, emphasising the importance of experience and wisdom in building a successful backroom team, especially looking back at Stoke's promotion to the Premier League with Gerry Francis on board.
