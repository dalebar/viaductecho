A 57-year-old man was found with serious injuries at an industrial estate in Salford and unfortunately passed away despite emergency services' efforts. Police received calls regarding a welfare concern on Devonshire Road, Worsley, early on Monday morning, February 9. They stated the incident isn't being treated as suspicious.
