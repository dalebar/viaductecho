A dramatic chase unfolded in south Manchester when police tried to stop a black Audi A3 flagged for no insurance. Instead of pulling over, the driver sped off, leading officers on a pursuit that ended outside Wythenshawe Hospital. After crashing into a taxi, the driver, a 19-year-old, was arrested for several offences including dangerous driving and driving without insurance. DC Katie Havens from Greater Manchester Police emphasised how this arrest not only brought a dangerous situation to a close safely but also helped tackle drug distribution in the community, showcasing the vital role of traffic enforcement in larger criminal investigations.
