Storm Goretti’s hitting the UK with strong winds, rain, snow, and sleet. A yellow snow warning’s in place for parts of Greater Manchester, including Bury, Oldham, and Stockport. There are also amber alerts for snow in the Midlands and Wales, and for wind in the South West. Travel could get tricky, with some rail lines closing later today. Manchester Airport is mostly running on schedule despite minor delays, though Jet2’s warned passengers about potential waiting times. Flights to Amsterdam Schiphol are facing disruptions due to ongoing severe weather, leading to numerous cancellations this week. Keep an eye on your flight status, as changes could happen last minute. Some flights from Manchester Airport on Thursday, January 8, include cancellations and estimated delays. Stay safe out there!
