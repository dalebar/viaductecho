It’s not the best start to a vital month for Manchester City. Pep Guardiola’s hoping this season doesn’t go off the rails like last year did. The club's missing key defenders Josko Gvardiol, Ruben Dias, and John Stones, and it looks like they won’t be back until next month at the earliest. Last season, City struggled without them and ended up without a major trophy for the first time in eight years, all thanks to a hefty injury list. Here’s to hoping things turn around quickly!
