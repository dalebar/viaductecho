A bloke has received a three-year ban from football matches after he punched a 13-year-old fan in the face during an unprovoked attack at a game. This football banning order means he won't be allowed at any regulated matches for the duration. For more updates, check out BBC Radio Merseyside on BBC Sounds and follow them on social media.
