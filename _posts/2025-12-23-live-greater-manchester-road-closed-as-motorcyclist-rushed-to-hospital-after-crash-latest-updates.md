Bridge Street in Golborne, near Wigan, is currently closed after a serious crash involving a motorcyclist and a car around 6.25am today. The motorcyclist was rushed to hospital with significant injuries, and no arrests have been made yet. Emergency services are on-site, managing the situation and conducting enquiries. A cordon is set up near the Lidl supermarket, and heavy traffic is building on surrounding roads. The closure affects the stretch between Kings Road and High Street, and updates will follow as investigations continue.
