Holly Wood, a savvy thrift shopper from Manchester, scored a fabulous pair of Stella McCartney shoes for just £10 in a charity shop, originally worth £325. At 39, she shares her finds with 29,000 TikTok followers and calls this her "designer find of the century." Her video showcasing the shoes has attracted 36,000 views, where she highlights their pristine condition, complete with the original tag. Viewers are loving her haul, with many praising the stunning shoes and expressing inspiration to hit charity shops themselves. Holly, a self-proclaimed “shopaholic,” has also bagged other impressive deals, including a £5 Coach bag and a vintage Vera Wang dress for £7. She's encouraging others to explore local charity shops, as they might uncover similar treasures!
