Stockport Georgians clinched a spot in the Edward Case Cup semi-finals with a thrilling 5-4 win against Maghull. The match saw plenty of drama, with Georgians initially struggling after conceding two goals in the first half. They bounced back, with Harry Lynn and Ollie McFadyen leveling the score before halftime. The second half featured more ups and downs, as Maghull regained the lead only for Georgians to fight back each time. A late-game surge saw McFadyen score the crucial winner in stoppage time, amidst a frantic finish. With solid performances and tactical changes, Georgians triumphed despite some nail-biting moments. The match ended 5-4 in favour of Georgians, securing their place in the semi-finals.
