A missing person alert has been issued for 17-year-old Bailey McCarron, last seen in Norris Green on February 18. He has links to south Manchester, particularly Didsbury and Withington. Bailey is about 5ft 6in tall, with mousy brown hair, and wearing a grey Canada Goose jacket, a cream ‘Stranger Things’ jumper, black combat trousers, and black/white Nike AirMax trainers. Police are asking anyone who spots him to call 999, emphasising the importance of ensuring his safety. Enquiries are ongoing, and any tips can also be reported via the police form or by contacting Merseyside Police.
