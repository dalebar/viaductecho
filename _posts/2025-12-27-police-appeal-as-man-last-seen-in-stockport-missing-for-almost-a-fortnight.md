Greater Manchester Police are seeking help from the public regarding a man named Alistair, last seen in Cheadle, Stockport, on December 14. Alistair is described as a 5ft10 white male with a medium build, blue eyes, and a bald head, wearing a light brown fur-lined jacket, brown hoodie, blue jeans, and brown shoes. If you have any information, please contact the police at 0161 856 9675, quoting log 1921 of 18/12/2025.
