Police are on the hunt for 29-year-old Thomas Edwards in relation to a stabbing investigation. Edwards is described as 6ft tall, with short brown hair, blue eyes, and tattoos on his hand and arm. He has a bruise under his right eye and has ties to Manchester, Accrington, Blackpool, and Essex. Residents are advised not to approach him but to contact the police if they see him. For any sightings or information, call 101 and quote log 1587 dated 13th February. Make sure to keep an eye out and stay safe!
