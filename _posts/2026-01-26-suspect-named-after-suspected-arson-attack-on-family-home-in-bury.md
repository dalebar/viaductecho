A man from Heywood, Jody Lynam, has been charged with arson after a fire at a family home in Bury on January 18. The blaze, which affected the dining room and kitchen, prompted a swift response from emergency services. Lynam, 45, is accused of intending to endanger life during the incident and has been remanded in custody, set to appear at Tameside Magistrates' Court today.
