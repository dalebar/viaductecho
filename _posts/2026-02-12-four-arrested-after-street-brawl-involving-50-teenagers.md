Four individuals were arrested following a brawl involving around 50 teenagers on Mount Road in Gorton. Police responded to the scene just before 5pm after receiving reports of the large fight. Witnesses noted a significant police presence, with two people arrested on suspicion of assault and two for affray. The group of youths was subsequently dispersed. A GMP spokesperson confirmed that no knives were found at the scene and the detainees were taken to Pendleton police station.
