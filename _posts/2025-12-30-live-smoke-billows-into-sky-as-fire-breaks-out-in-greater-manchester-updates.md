A fire has erupted this morning in the Tameside area of Greater Manchester, with thick, black smoke visible from Audenshaw. The blaze seems to have started near the Shepley Industrial Estate North, and locals started noticing the smoke around 7.30am. People in surrounding areas, including Denton and Hyde, can also see the smoke. The Manchester Evening News has reached out to the Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service for more info, and crews are currently on-site. Stay tuned to our live blog for updates!
