Harry Styles is set to perform at the 2026 Brit Awards, showcasing tracks from his new album, *Kiss All The Time, Disco, Occasionally*, due out on March 6. The show will take place at Manchester's Co-op Live on February 28, marking the first time the ceremony is held in the city in nearly 50 years. Styles, who previously took home four awards in 2023, will share the stage with Olivia Dean. 

This performance comes as he kicks off his *Together, Together* global tour this summer. Harry, originally from Holmes Chapel in Cheshire, gained fame in One Direction and has since enjoyed a successful solo career with numbers one albums and singles, notably *As It Was* and *Sign Of The Times*. His debut album arrived in 2017, followed by *Fine Line* and *Harry's House*, further cementing his status as the most successful solo artist from the group.
