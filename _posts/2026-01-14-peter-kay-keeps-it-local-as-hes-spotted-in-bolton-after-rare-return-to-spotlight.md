Peter Kay was recently spotted in Bolton, enjoying lunch as he promotes the final leg of his successful "Better Late Than Never" tour. After kicking off in 2022, he's announced his last shows, with all profits going to cancer charities. The tour has been a smashing success, breaking records at venues like Manchester’s AO Arena and The O2 in London. He surprised fans with the news during an appearance on BBC's The One Show, expressing his gratitude for the overwhelming support throughout his career. The last tour dates include stops in various UK cities and Dublin, wrapping up on August 8, 2026. Charities benefitting from the profits include Children With Cancer UK, Teenage Cancer Trust, and more, highlighting his commitment to giving back. Peter was keeping a low profile in a cap and black outfit while grabbing a bite in his hometown, proving he’s still all about the local vibe.
