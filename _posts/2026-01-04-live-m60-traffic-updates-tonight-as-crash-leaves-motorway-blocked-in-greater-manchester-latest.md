The M60 motorway in Greater Manchester is currently blocked due to a crash between Junctions 16 and 17 near Prestwich. Traffic has been halted since shortly after 7pm, with emergency services on the scene. Drivers are advised to find alternative routes and expect delays. The collision has caused significant damage to a vehicle, and all traffic is being held in a full closure. Inrix reports queues stretching back towards Junction 15. Stay tuned for updates as the situation develops.
