Manchester City is set to sign Crystal Palace captain Marc Guehi for £20m. Although City boss Pep Guardiola was tight-lipped when asked about the move, Crystal Palace's manager, Glasner, praised Guehi's professionalism amidst the transfer buzz. However, he acknowledged that once a player is keen to leave, a transfer is likely to go through.
