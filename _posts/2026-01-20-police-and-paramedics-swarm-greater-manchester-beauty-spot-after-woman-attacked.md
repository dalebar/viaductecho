A woman was attacked in Boggart Hole Clough, Blackley, on the afternoon of January 19, around 3.30pm. Police and paramedics rushed to the scene, and the area was temporarily cordoned off for investigations. It's reported that she was assaulted with a weapon, but thankfully her injuries are not life-threatening. She has since been taken to hospital. Authorities are looking into the incident, but as of now, no arrests have been made. Greater Manchester Police confirmed they are continuing their enquiries.
