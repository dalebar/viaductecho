Aribim Pepple clinched a nail-biting victory for Plymouth Argyle with a last-minute goal, securing a 4-3 win over Bristol Rovers to move into the Vertu Trophy quarter-finals. In another surprising turn, Swindon Town, sitting high in League Two, triumphed over League One's Luton Town with a 2-1 win at Kenilworth Road. Meanwhile, veteran striker Billy Sharp netted two goals for Doncaster Rovers, leading them to a 3-1 victory over Fleetwood Town and ending their Wembley dreams this season.
