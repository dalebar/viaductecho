Manchester United captain Bruno Fernandes expressed disappointment upon discovering the club was open to his departure when Al-Hilal made a substantial offer. He admitted that it "hurt" to know he wasn't fully wanted but emphasized that it made him "sad" more than anything. Fernandes highlighted his commitment, saying he's always available and gives his best, making it clear that he feels he shouldn't be criticised.
