Greater Manchester Police are searching for 14-year-old Lily, who went missing in Stockport town centre on Saturday, January 3, around 5:15pm. She's described as a white female, about 5' 5'', with long straight red/purple hair. At the time she went missing, she was wearing a black Hoodrich puffer coat with a fur trim, a black top, grey Nike yoga pants, and pink and white Nike trainers. If anyone has information about Lily's whereabouts, please reach out to the police at 101 or call 999 in an emergency, quoting log 2333 of 03/01/2026.
