Manchester United fans have a catchy tune for Michael Carrick, featuring the line "hard to believe it's not Scholes." This season, Carrick's been a familiar face at the club. As a manager, he kept his polite and friendly manner, avoiding unnecessary drama. Previously, he stepped in as United's manager after Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's departure in November 2021, taking the reins from his assistant coach role. During his short stint, he led the team in three games, securing two wins and a draw, including a notable 3-2 victory against Arsenal.
