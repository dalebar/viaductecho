A 45-year-old man tragically died after being hit by a car while crossing the road in Ardwick in the early hours of Sunday. Despite emergency services' best efforts, he succumbed to his injuries shortly after the incident. The collision happened outside a block of flats near Ardwick Green Park, and the driver remained at the scene; no arrests have been made. Greater Manchester Police are now appealing for witnesses or anyone with CCTV, dashcam, or doorbell footage to come forward. They are working to investigate the full circumstances of the situation and have informed the man's family, who are being supported by specialist officers. For information, the public can reach out to the Serious Collision Investigation Unit or contact Crimestoppers anonymously. No further details have been provided, and the man's name has not been released.
