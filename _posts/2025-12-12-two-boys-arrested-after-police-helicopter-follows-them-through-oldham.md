Two boys were arrested in Oldham after a police helicopter helped track them down following a brief pursuit. One boy is held on multiple charges, including burglary and dangerous driving, while the other was arrested for theft of a vehicle and has since been bailed. Police used a stinger device to stop a Mazda during the operation.
