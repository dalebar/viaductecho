Police in Manchester are urgently seeking help to locate a missing woman named Amy, who was last seen at North Manchester General Hospital on December 11. The 48-year-old is described as a white female, around 5ft 8in tall, of medium build, wearing a grey hoodie, white t-shirt, black shorts, and brown shoes. Officers are increasingly concerned for her wellbeing and are asking anyone with information about her whereabouts to contact them at 0161 856 7821 or 101, quoting log 3151 of 11/12/25.
