Manchester Piccadilly station was evacuated around 10am on Wednesday due to a suspicious package. Thankfully, it turned out to be non-suspicious, and the situation was resolved. British Transport Police implemented a precautionary cordon, but everything is back to normal now. Passengers might experience some disruptions, with most affected services seeing delays under 10 minutes, while some trains may face delays of up to 30 minutes.
