Marple, a charming town in Stockport, has recently earned the top spot in Garrington Property Finder's national ranking for best places to live in the UK. Known for its "understated charm," it offers stunning natural beauty with access to the countryside and plenty of walking routes. Just 25 minutes from Manchester city centre, Marple combines a village feel with city amenities, making it popular among families and professionals seeking a better lifestyle. 

Local estate agents highlight strong demand for various property types, with families and couples flocking to the area for its community spirit, independent shops, and quality schools. Average house prices are above the national average, but Marple's appeal lies in its blend of green spaces and good transport links.  Residents value the town for its picturesque setting and vibrant local life, contributing to its growing reputation and desirability.
