A driver arrested after an elderly man was struck by a car in Bury has been 'de-arrested', according to police. The incident occurred on Haymarket Street at the Angouleme Way junction on November 25, prompting emergency services to close the road. The elderly man was taken to hospital with injuries not considered life-threatening. Initially, the driver was suspected of drug-driving, but police later confirmed he has been released. No further details on the incident are available at this time. Bury Council had alerted the public about road closures and potential delays due to the collision.
