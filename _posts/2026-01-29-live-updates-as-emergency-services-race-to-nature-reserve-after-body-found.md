Emergency crews are responding to a tragic incident at Healey Dell Nature Reserve in Rochdale, where a man's body was found in the water. The police received reports of the body around 9:40 am and confirmed its recovery later in the day. A cordon is in place, and emergency services, including police, firefighters, and paramedics, are on the scene conducting enquiries. The area is popular with walkers and runners, but visitors are being turned away as investigations continue. Updates on the situation are being shared live.
