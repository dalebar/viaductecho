Ruben Amorim was spotted having lunch in Wilmslow with his wife, Maria Joao Diogo, and agent Raul Costa just a day after being sacked by Manchester United. The former manager appeared in good spirits, spending an hour and 20 minutes at a local restaurant and even exchanging pleasantries with a photographer. His dismissal followed a rocky 14 months with the club, marked by tense moments, notably after a recent draw with Leeds where he expressed frustrations about his role and the team's performance. Despite previous comments hinting at potentially leaving when his contract expired, his clashes with United's staff and a reluctance to adjust his tactics led to his quick dismissal.
