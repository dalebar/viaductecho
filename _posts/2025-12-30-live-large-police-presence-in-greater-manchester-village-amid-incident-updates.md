There’s quite a large police presence in Worsley, Salford this afternoon, with at least five emergency vehicles spotted on Worsley Road near Mill Brow and The Delph bar. The specifics of the incident haven’t been disclosed yet, but Greater Manchester Police have been asked for more info. Keep an eye on the live blog for updates as they come in. The police are positioned close to a path leading into Worsley Woods too.
