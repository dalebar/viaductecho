Antoine Semenyo is in advanced discussions about a possible January move to Manchester City, with the decision about his future looming. Liverpool's also sniffing around, especially after Alexander Isak's injury, but it'll depend on whether they ramp up their interest. Financially, it seems all potential suitors are prepared to offer him similar packages. Semenyo's choice will likely hinge on his personal sporting goals. Unlike last January's spending spree due to injuries, City is approaching this signing with a more stable strategy.
