A 15-year-old boy was stabbed and injured in Salford, Greater Manchester, on New Year's Day. Emergency services rushed to Lower Broughton Road after reports of the assault. The victim sustained minor injuries and was taken to hospital for treatment. Two other boys, aged 14 and 15, were arrested on suspicion of affray but have since been released on bail. Greater Manchester Police confirmed the details, noting the incident involved a police van and an ambulance at the scene.
