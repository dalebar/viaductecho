Tributes are pouring in for 23-year-old Peter-Lee Ian Noctor, who sadly died in an incident at Ashton-under-Lyne train station on November 6. Emergency services rushed to the scene after reports of a casualty on the tracks, but despite paramedics' efforts, he was pronounced dead. The police have stated that the incident is not considered suspicious. An inquest into his death will be held at South Manchester Coroner's Court. His funeral took place in Dukinfield on December 2, and heartfelt messages have flooded social media, with friends remembering him as a beautiful young man taken far too soon. One tribute expressed deep sorrow, saying, "Such a young life taken too soon," while another highlighted his "cheeky smile." Overall, the community is mourning the loss of a kind soul who touched many lives.
