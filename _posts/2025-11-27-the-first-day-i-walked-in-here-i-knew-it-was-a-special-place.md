The Manchester Evening News has been a vital part of the city for 157 years, evolving from a traditional newspaper into a multi-platform brand that connects with its readers. Now, they're launching Manchester Evening News Premium, offering a refined journalism experience with fewer ads for a subscription fee. The goal is to ensure sustainable, quality reporting that tells Manchester’s stories, holding authority accountable while also celebrating the region's culture. The editor, Sarah Lester, reflects on her journey from a young reporter to editor, highlighting significant investigations and campaigns that have made a real impact, such as promoting new laws for housing safety and supporting local causes. With trust in journalism increasingly vital amid misinformation, MEN aims to provide accurate reporting and deeper engagement with the community. Subscriptions start at £4.99 monthly, with the first month at just £1. By subscribing, readers help sustain crucial local journalism.
