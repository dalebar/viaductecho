Primark has acknowledged an incident involving a young boy who got his hand stuck in a lift at their Stockport store in Greater Manchester on December 12. Shoppers and staff quickly came to the boy's aid after hearing a terrifying scream and chaos ensued in the store. A witness described the boy's hand as being trapped in the open lift doors, and another shopper used a metal clothing rail to help free him while contacting emergency services.
