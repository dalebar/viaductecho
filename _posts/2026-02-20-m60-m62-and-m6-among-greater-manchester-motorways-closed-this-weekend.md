This weekend, there’s gonna be some motorway closures around Greater Manchester from Friday night to Monday morning, particularly affecting the M60, M62, and M6. National Highways has laid out a detailed list of closures, mainly happening overnight. Key highlights include:

- M6 Southbound: Junction 28 exit slip (10 PM to 5 AM)
- M61 closures affecting multiple junctions (varied times from 9 PM to 6 AM)
- M62 Eastbound and Westbound closures at Junction 11 (9 PM to 5 AM)
- M67 and M56 also seeing various closures (between 8 PM and 6 AM)

Drivers should keep this in mind when planning their journeys. It’s a good idea to check your route ahead of time! Stay safe on the roads, everyone!
