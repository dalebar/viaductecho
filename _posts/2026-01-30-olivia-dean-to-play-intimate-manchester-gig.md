Olivia Dean is set to perform at a charity gig in Manchester's Albert Hall on 26 February as part of Brits Week, supporting War Child, which aids children affected by conflict. Other acts include Spiritualized, performing in London on 18 February, and The K's at Gorilla in Manchester shortly after. Harry Styles will also be in Manchester for the Brit Awards on 28 February. Dean, who has two UK top 10 albums and a chart-topping single, is generating a buzz ahead of her performance. Spiritualized, led by J Spaceman, boasts three top 10 albums, including the renowned *Ladies And Gentlemen We Are Floating In Space*. Clare Sanders-Wright from War Child expressed gratitude for the artists' generosity, highlighting the urgency of helping children in conflict zones.
