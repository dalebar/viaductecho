Four survivors from a horrific crash in Bolton remain in hospital almost three weeks after the incident on January 11, which resulted in four fatalities. The crash involved a red Seat Leon and a Citroen C4 Picasso taxi, claiming the lives of the drivers and two teenage passengers. Five passengers were rushed to hospital; while four from the taxi still require care, one from the Seat has been discharged. The tragic event has deeply affected the families of victims, with emotional tributes highlighting the lives lost. A fundraiser for the survivors has raised Â£50,000 to aid their immediate expenses. Inquests into the deaths are underway. The police investigation continues, with the driver of the Seat reportedly speeding at the time of the crash. Authorities urge witnesses to come forward with any information or footage related to the incident.
