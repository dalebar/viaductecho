Police are investigating a shooting incident in Salford where a woman in her 20s was injured. The event occurred last Thursday evening at a property on Wally Square, with a suspect approaching on an electric bike before firing a weapon. A 22-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of firearms offences, while two others, aged 24 and 28, have been released on bail. The Greater Manchester Police are urging anyone with information, including CCTV or dashcam footage, to come forward. Detective Inspector Paul Costello stressed the seriousness of firearms discharges and the commitment to understanding the incident's circumstances. If you have information, you can contact the police at 101 or via their online service, quoting log 2932 of 04/12/25, or reach out to Crimestoppers anonymously at 0800 555 111.
