It's a busy travel time in the UK as folks head off for Christmas holidays, with millions hitting the roads and skies. The RAC reports around 4.2 million journeys expected on Christmas Eve alone! Manchester Airport is preparing for its busiest Christmas yet, anticipating 1.5 million passengers from December 19 to January 4. The peak day will be Sunday, December 28, when 100,000 travellers are set to fly. However, with busy travel times come possible flight delays due to weather, staffing, or air traffic control issues, so it's wise to check flight statuses regularly. Recent cancellations include a 1.55pm SAS flight to Stockholm, along with several easyJet and Aer Lingus flights facing delays. Stay updated and have a lovely time holidaying or visiting loved ones!
