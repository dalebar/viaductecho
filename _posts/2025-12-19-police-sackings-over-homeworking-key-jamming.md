Four staff members from Greater Manchester Police have been dismissed and banned from policing over "key-jamming," a tactic that falsely makes it look like they're on duty. Deputy Chief Constable Terry Woods praised the anti-corruption unit's efforts in addressing this misconduct, stating it's vital for maintaining integrity within GMP.
