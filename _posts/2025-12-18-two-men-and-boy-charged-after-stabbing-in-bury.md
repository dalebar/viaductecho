Three individuals, including two 23-year-old brothers from Radcliffe and a 16-year-old boy, have been charged following a stabbing incident in Bury on December 16. Initially, four people were arrested, but after further investigations, three were charged with violent disorder, with one brother also facing charges for possessing a bladed article. They are set to appear at Manchester and Salford Magistrates Court. Additionally, a second 16-year-old arrested has been bailed pending further inquiries, while three more men, aged 19, 29, and 30, have also been taken into custody regarding the incident.
