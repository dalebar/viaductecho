Drew McCormick, a young man in his 20s, was fatally stabbed in Wythenshawe, with a 19-year-old suspect arrested for murder. Witnesses reported hearing a woman pleading before the incident, which occurred late on Thursday night. Neighbours expressed shock at the violence in what they considered a quiet area, with many unaware of the severity until morning. Police cordoned off the scene on Southwick Road, conducting investigations and gathering evidence. Friends remembered Drew as a kind, funny, and loving person, who brought joy to their lives, with heartfelt tributes highlighting his character. The police are urging anyone with information to come forward.
