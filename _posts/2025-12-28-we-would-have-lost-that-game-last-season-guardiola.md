Pep Guardiola has cautioned his squad about staying fit during the festive season, but really, there's not much call for concern at Manchester City. He feels the team is back in form after a vital win. They were missing a few key players like Jeremy Doku, John Stones, Oscar Bobb, and Mateo Kovacic due to injuries, plus Omar Marmoush and Rayan Ait-Nouri are off on international duty for the Africa Cup of Nations.
