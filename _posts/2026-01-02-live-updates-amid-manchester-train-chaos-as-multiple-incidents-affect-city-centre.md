Passengers in Manchester are facing disruptions today due to multiple incidents affecting trains through Manchester Oxford Road. Services from Northern and TransPennine Express are particularly hit, with delays and cancellations anticipated throughout the morning. National Rail reports that congestion has built up and lines are disrupted, with some delays extending up to 30 minutes and lasting until around 11:00. Northern passengers can use their tickets on Metrolink services between Manchester Piccadilly and Manchester Airport until 10:30. Additionally, TransPennine Express services from Buckshaw Village, Leeds, and Cleethorpes are also experiencing delays. Keep an eye out for live updates on the situation!
