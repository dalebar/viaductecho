Derbyshire Police are deeply worried about a missing woman named Gemma, 43, last seen in Stockport on December 30 around 7:20pm. She was reported missing from Chesterfield Road, Chesterfield earlier that day. When she disappeared, Gemma was wearing a black puffer jacket and black joggers with colourful writing. Relatives say she was spotted in Stockport wearing a denim jacket, hat, and scarf. Gemma is described as 5ft 3ins tall, of a large build, with shoulder-length blonde hair featuring a blue streak. The police urge anyone with information to reach out, referencing 569 of December 30, through their website, Facebook page, or by calling 101. You can also contact CrimeStoppers anonymously at 0800 555 111 or via their website.
