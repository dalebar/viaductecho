Community leaders expressed deep concern over the rising antisemitism and highlighted the threat posed to the local Jewish community, especially following a recent attack at Heaton Park. Marc Levy, CEO of the Jewish Representative Council, emphasised the importance of addressing the root causes of radicalisation rather than simply increasing security measures. He reinforced their commitment to resilience and coexistence within Manchester, noting that the community has contributed significantly to the region for generations. Two others connected to the plot also received sentences, and the community continues to cope with the challenges posed by such extremist threats while striving to maintain their vibrant presence in the area.
