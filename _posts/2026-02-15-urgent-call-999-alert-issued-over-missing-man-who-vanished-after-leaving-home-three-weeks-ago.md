Police are urgently seeking help in finding Ajani Campbell, a 40-year-old man missing from Preston since January 24. Last seen in Ashton-on-Ribble around 10:30pm, he has connections to Manchester and London. Police are increasingly concerned for his welfare and have distributed a recent photo to aid in the search. If anyone spots Ajani, they are urged to call 999 right away. For previous sightings or information, people can reach out to 101, quoting log 1159 from January 25.
