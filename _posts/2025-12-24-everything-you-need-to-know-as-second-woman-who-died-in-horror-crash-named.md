A tragic road accident in Bolton has claimed the lives of two women, named as 57-year-old Dawn Reed and 59-year-old Vicki Attenborough. The incident occurred on Scout Road when they were struck by a VW Polo. A 19-year-old driver has been arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving. Vicki, who recently ran for MP for Bolton West, was remembered fondly by her family for her commitment to social justice and charitable work. They highlighted her caring nature and the void her passing has left in many lives. Anyone with information or footage regarding the incident is encouraged to contact Greater Manchester Police or Crimestoppers.
