Drivers on the M62 near Rochdale are facing significant delays due to a partial blockage following an accident at junction 20. Traffic service Inrix reported that queues stretch back to junction 18 for Simister Island, causing a right hassle for commuters. However, the accident has now been cleared, and live coverage of the incident has ended.
