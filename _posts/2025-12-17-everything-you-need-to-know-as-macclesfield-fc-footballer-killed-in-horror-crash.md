Ethan McLeod, a 21-year-old striker for Macclesfield FC, tragically died in a car crash on the M1 after a game against Bedford Town. The incident occurred around 10:40pm when his Mercedes hit a barrier near Milton Keynes. Despite efforts from emergency services and bystanders, he was pronounced dead at the scene. Macclesfield FC has paid tribute to Ethan, highlighting his talent, respect within the team, and inspirational work ethic. The club extended their condolences and support to his family, friends, and teammates during this difficult time. Northamptonshire Police are seeking witnesses and dash-cam footage to investigate the collision.
