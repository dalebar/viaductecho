High Peak Comps is running a massive Christmas prize draw with a whopping £100,000 at stake—its biggest giveaway yet! Tickets are just 99p each, and you can buy up to 3,000 to boost your chances. The draw wraps up on Christmas Day, and the winner will be announced live at 1pm on their Facebook page. Alongside this exciting competition, they’re known for daily giveaways and community support, recently donating an air fryer for a charity gala to benefit the Stockport NHS. Established in 2020, they’ve already given away £10 million in prizes and continue to champion local causes. If you’re interested in their competitions or have a community project needing support, get in touch! Keep an eye on their Facebook for updates and have a cheeky look at the other contests while you’re at it!
