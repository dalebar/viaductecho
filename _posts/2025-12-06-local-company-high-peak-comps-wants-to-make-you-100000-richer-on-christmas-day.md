High Peak Comps, a business in Buxton, is set to make one lucky winner £100,000 richer on Christmas Day with their biggest ever prize draw. Scheduled for 1pm on December 25, the entry fee is just 99p per ticket, with a total of 199,999 tickets available. This year’s prize is £25,000 more than last year’s draw. The winner will be announced live and will receive the money within 24 hours. High Peak Comps has been active in supporting local charities, donating to schools and healthcare units. They also offer various tech prizes, including an iPhone 17 Pro and a Ninja Coffee Machine, both with low entry fees. For anyone eager to enter the draw, tickets are available online. Good luck and Merry Christmas from High Peak Comps!
