Flood warnings are back in effect across Greater Manchester after last New Year's Eve's heavy rain caused widespread flooding, affecting areas like Cheadle Hulme, Gatley, and Stockport's Meadow Mill. Doreen McCracken from Gatley shared her ordeal of months dealing with flooded interiors. The Met Office had previously put out an amber alert for the downpour, and the Environment Agency issued a flood warning just before the celebrations began.
