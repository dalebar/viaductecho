Music legend Mani's funeral took place today at Manchester Cathedral, attended by stars like Liam Gallagher, Gary Neville, and Paul Weller. Mani, who died peacefully at 63 due to respiratory issues linked to emphysema, was remembered fondly by family and friends. His coffin, adorned with John Squire's iconic 'Bye Bye, Badman' artwork, was carried by Liam and former bandmates, including Ian Brown and Bobby Gillespie, who shared heartfelt tributes. The gathering outside the cathedral applauded as the cortege arrived, showcasing the impact Mani had on many.
