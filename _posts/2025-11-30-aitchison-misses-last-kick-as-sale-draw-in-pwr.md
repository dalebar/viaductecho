Saracens took the lead in the Premiership Women's Rugby table after a dominant 57-5 victory over Trailfinders in a London derby, equal on points with Gloucester Hartpury. Francesca McGhie kicked things off for Trailfinders early, but Saracens swiftly equalised with a try from Sydney Gregson. Meanwhile, at Franklin's Gardens, Leicester Tigers scored first against Loughborough Lightning with Georgie Lingham finding the try line just four minutes into the match.
