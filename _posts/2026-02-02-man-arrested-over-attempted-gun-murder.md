A 36-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of attempted murder and possession of a firearm after a gun was fired in Salford on Sunday evening. The incident occurred on St Joseph's Drive, leading to a stand-off with armed police. Thankfully, no injuries were reported. Greater Manchester Police are treating the situation seriously, vowing zero tolerance for firearms and intimidation in the area. The investigation is ongoing, with officers still present in the area to ensure public safety.
