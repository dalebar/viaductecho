Chelsea's boss Enzo Maresca has dismissed talk about him possibly taking over from Pep Guardiola at Manchester City, calling it "100% speculation." His remarks follow a tough few days after Chelsea's recent 2-0 win over Everton, which he described as his "worst 48 hours" since taking the helm. There seems to be a strained relationship between Maresca and the club's higher-ups that's still causing him a bit of grief.
