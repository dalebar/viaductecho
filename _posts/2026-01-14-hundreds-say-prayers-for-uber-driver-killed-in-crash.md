Hundreds turned out to mourn a taxi driver who tragically died in a head-on crash in Greater Manchester, which also claimed the lives of three teenagers: Muhammad Danyaal Asghar Ali, Farhan Patel, and Mohammed Jibrael Mukhtar. The taxi driver, Masrob, had dedicated ten years to his profession and was well-loved for his friendly nature and lack of involvement in trouble. His death has deeply affected the community, highlighting the impact he had on those around him.
