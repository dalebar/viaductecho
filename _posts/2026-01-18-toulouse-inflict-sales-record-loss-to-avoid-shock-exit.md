Toulouse pulled off a stunning performance in the Investec Champions Cup, thrashing Sale Sharks with 11 tries, marking Sale's heaviest defeat. Sale chose to rest key players like George Ford and Tom Curry, which may not have changed the outcome, considering Toulouse's dominant attack. After Durban Sharks' victory over Clermont Auvergne, Toulouse slipped out of the last-16 qualification spots. The match was officiated by Andrew Brace from Ireland.
