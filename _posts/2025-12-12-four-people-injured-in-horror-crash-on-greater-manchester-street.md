A serious crash on Boyds Walk in Dukinfield left four men injured, including a pedestrian, on Thursday evening. The incident, which involved a car and a van, occurred around 5pm. Emergency services, including a significant police and fire presence, responded quickly. Two of the injured men were hospitalised for treatment, while a third went on his own. Fortunately, their injuries aren't considered life-changing or life-threatening. The pedestrian was treated for minor injuries at the scene. Boyds Walk was closed until about 10pm as investigations took place. Further details from Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service are awaited.
