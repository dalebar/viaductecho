Ah, 2016 – what a year! From Leicester City’s Premier League shocker to Britain’s Brexit vote, it was a memorable one. Recently, social media’s been buzzing as TikTok users dive into nostalgia, with a massive rise in searches for ’2016’.

In Manchester, the music scene was electric: Beyonce’s epic gig at Old Trafford and the Stone Roses' last shows at the Etihad got crowds buzzing. Justin Bieber had his fair share of fun around town too! 

Tragically, we lost legends like David Bowie and Prince, with city artists paying tribute through stunning murals. The year also saw important events like one of Manchester’s first major Black Lives Matter marches and Jeremy Corbyn’s rise in politics.

Manchester United hired Jose Mourinho, while City claimed the League Cup. Who could forget the Pokémon Go frenzy that had everyone out in Piccadilly Gardens? And let’s not overlook the viral Snapchat dog filter! A proper walk down memory lane, that.
