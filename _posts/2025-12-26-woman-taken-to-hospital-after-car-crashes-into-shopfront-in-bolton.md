A woman was hospitalised today after a car crashed into a hair salon in Bolton around 1.30pm. The car smashed through the shop front, causing significant damage. Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service responded with fire engines from Bolton and a Technical Response Unit from Ashton, quickly securing the scene. Firefighters spent about an hour ensuring the area was safe. Police were also on site to assist.
