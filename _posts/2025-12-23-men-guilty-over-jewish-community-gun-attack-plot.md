Two men, Walid Saadaoui from Abram, Wigan, and Amar Hussein, were found guilty of plotting a terror attack aimed at the Jewish community in Greater Manchester. Their preparations spanned from 13 December 2023 to 9 May 2024, with Saadaoui being arrested in a Bolton hotel car park on 8 May 2023.
