Eddie Howe, Newcastle United's head coach, has expressed his contentment at the club, brushing off rumours tying him to the Manchester United position. He also touched on the previous tensions with sporting director Paul Mitchell, who departed after less than a year. Everything seems to be on solid ground for Howe at Newcastle.
