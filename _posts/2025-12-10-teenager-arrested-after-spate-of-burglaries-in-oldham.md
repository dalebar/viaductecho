A 19-year-old has been arrested in connection with several early morning burglaries in Oldham, following reports from the Fitton Hill area on December 1. Greater Manchester Police have been busy reviewing CCTV footage and interviewing witnesses to make this arrest. DS Parkin from the Neighbourhood Crime team reassures locals by emphasising their commitment to tackling crime and the serious impact burglaries have on both victims and the wider community. Investigations are ongoing, and anyone with information is encouraged to reach out to the police via their LiveChat or Crimestoppers.
