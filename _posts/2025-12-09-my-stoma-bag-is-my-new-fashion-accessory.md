Lucy proudly embraces her stoma bag, calling it her "bag for life" and a trendy accessory. Inspired by her own experience after emergency surgery, she organised a charity catwalk to raise awareness about living with a stoma, showcasing models who confidently displayed their bags. Fellow participant Nikita Patel, also a stoma bag user, echoed Lucy's hope that attendees would feel empowered to celebrate their differences. The event was all about breaking the stigma and embracing individuality.
