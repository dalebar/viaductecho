This weekend in Stockport is buzzing with events, courtesy of EatGoodWest UK and Waterside Hotel & Leisure Club. Here’s a quick rundown of what’s happening:

- **Honda Dreamy Sales Event** on Fri 13 Feb at Swansway Honda Stockport, featuring up to £2,000 off the e:Ny1 and £1,000 off other models.
  
- **Valentine's Day Fun** at Merseyway Shopping Centre on Sat 14 Feb from 11am, with activities like a 360-degree camera opportunity, meeting Lola Love Heart, and Valentine crafts—ideal for family outings!

- **"When Harry Met Sally" screening** at Stockport Plaza on Sat 14 Feb at 2.30pm and 7.30pm, a perfect cinema choice for Valentine’s Day.

- **Disney Pin Trade & Buy** at Titan Pops on Sun 15 Feb at 2pm, welcoming all Disney pin enthusiasts to trade and connect.

- **Pyramid Concert Orchestra's 'Whistle Stop Tour'** on Sun 15 Feb at 3.30pm at Stockport Plaza, showcasing international music treasures.
