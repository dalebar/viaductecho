Arighi Bianchi’s biggest-ever Winter Sale is on in Macclesfield, featuring fantastic savings on furniture, interiors, and home accessories. You can snag up to 30% off in-store and online on items like sofas, dining tables, luxury mattresses, and designer brands such as Cattelan Italia and Tempur. The sale runs from December 27 to January 18, with special Early Bird Offers on key items. Spend £1,200 in-store to get a £50 voucher for CASA Accessories, or £100 with £2,500 spend. There are also tempting deals on flooring, wallpaper, and more. With flexible 0% APR finance over 24 months available, upgrading your space is more affordable than ever. Don’t miss out—check their website and socials for the latest offers. Whether you’re popping down to The Silk Road or shopping online, it’s a fab time to refresh your home!
