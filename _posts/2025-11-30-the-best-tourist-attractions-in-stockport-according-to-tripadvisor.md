Stockport’s got a bit of everything, from stunning scenery to thrilling performances! Topping Tripadvisor’s list of attractions is the Stockport Air Raid Shelters, a historical site giving a peek into WWII life. Next up is the Avro Heritage Museum, perfect for aircraft buffs, featuring flight simulators and a café with great views.

The Hat Works Museum is the UK’s only hat-dedicated museum, showcasing quirky exhibits and famous hats. For live entertainment, Stockport Plaza offers shows and music events galore. Reddish Vale Farm is a lovely spot for animal lovers, complete with a soft play area and beautiful park walks.

Etherow Country Park, noted for its vast greenery, is one of the UK's oldest country parks, while the historical Marple Locks makes for a lovely walking and cycling spot. Staircase House, dating back to 1460, gives insights into Stockport’s past, as does the Tudor Bramall Hall. Finally, Stockport Museum brings 10,000 years of local history to life. There's plenty to keep everyone entertained in this charming borough!
