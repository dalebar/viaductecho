Ahead of his first match back as Manchester United's head coach, Michael Carrick described Old Trafford as "a magical place". Despite retiring over ten years ago, Carrick still holds a season ticket alongside his family. He earned praise from past captains Gary Neville and Roy Keane, who had been critical of his appointment. In their latest game, United showcased solid performance despite having only 32% possession, successfully defending against City and restricting them to a mere 0.45 xG.
