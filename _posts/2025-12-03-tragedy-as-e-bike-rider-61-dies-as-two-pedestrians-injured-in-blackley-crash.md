A tragic incident occurred in Blackley, resulting in the death of a 61-year-old e-bike rider following a crash on Rochdale Road. The collision, which happened around 8:45 PM on December 2, involved the e-bike hitting two pedestrians, aged 48 and 50, who suffered minor injuries and are currently receiving treatment. The roads in the area reopened early the next morning after being closed for the emergency response. Greater Manchester Police are now appealing for witnesses and information, particularly from anyone who might have dashcam footage or saw the events leading up to the incident. Those with information can reach out to the Serious Collision Investigation Unit or contact Crime Stoppers anonymously.
