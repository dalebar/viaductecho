A cordon’s been set up near a shopping precinct in Middleton, specifically around the car park on Chapel Street in Rhodes. Witnesses spotted a blue forensics tent inside this area. The exact details of the incident haven’t been confirmed yet, but Greater Manchester Police (GMP) are investigating three reported assaults. Locals have mentioned seeing plenty of police presence in the vicinity. The Manchester Evening News is following the situation and is awaiting an official statement from GMP for more info. Live updates will be provided as they come in.
