James Morton, 33, is set to face trial this summer for the murder of 45-year-old Gloria De Lazzari in Bolton. Police responded to an incident at his Kempston Gardens residence on January 19. Morton has been remanded in custody and his case was moved to Manchester Crown Court, where a plea hearing is planned for April and the trial for July. Gloria's mother and ex-husband paid heartfelt tributes, describing her as a fantastic person, devoted daughter, and a gentle soul who brought kindness and warmth to everyone around her. They expressed deep sorrow over her loss, emphasising the love and goodness she shared with others.
