Police are requesting help after a burglary on Clifton Park Road on 18 November, between 1-3pm. A man allegedly broke into a home, stealing jewellery and personal items. They’re keen to identify a man in a photo, believing he could aid in their investigation. If you’ve got any info, ring police on 101 and quote log 2389 from 18/11/2025.
