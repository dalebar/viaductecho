The chilly weather in Greater Manchester and the UK shows no signs of easing on Tuesday. Despite the cold snap, Manchester Airport is running smoothly, with most flights on schedule, though a few KLM flights to Amsterdam have been cancelled due to severe weather at Schiphol. The specific cancellations include flights at 5.50am, 9.05am, and 1.40pm. The airport is well-prepared for winter conditions, utilising over 30 snow-clearing vehicles and special forecasting systems to minimise disruptions.
