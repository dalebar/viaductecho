A blind student, Abdul Eneser, had a close call with a train after falling onto the tracks due to a lack of station staff to assist him. He had pre-booked Passenger Assist but arrived later than expected at Manchester Piccadilly due to delays. Network Rail issued an apology, expressing regret over his experience.
