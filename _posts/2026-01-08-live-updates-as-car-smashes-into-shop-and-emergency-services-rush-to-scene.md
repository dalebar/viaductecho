Emergency services are on the scene following a serious crash in Gorton this evening, January 8. A car reportedly smashed into Dixa Hair and Beauty Salon on Hyde Road, overturning and leaving debris scattered about. While the car has been removed, road closures remain in effect, causing heavy traffic in the area. Reports indicate that the driver left the scene, and police are working to locate them. Thankfully, no injuries have been reported. A large emergency presence is on-site, including firefighters clearing the debris and marked police vehicles managing the situation. Updates will be provided as more information comes in.
