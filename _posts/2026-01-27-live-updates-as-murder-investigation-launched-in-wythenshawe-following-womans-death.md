A murder investigation is underway in Wythenshawe after the tragic death of a woman in her 30s on Lulworth Gardens. Police responded to reports of a disturbance around 8.30am on January 27, where they found the woman unresponsive. Despite attempts at CPR, she was sadly pronounced dead at the scene. A 44-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is currently in custody.

Superintendent Gayle Brister reassured the community that there's no wider risk to the public and that officers are diligently working to uncover the details of the incident.
