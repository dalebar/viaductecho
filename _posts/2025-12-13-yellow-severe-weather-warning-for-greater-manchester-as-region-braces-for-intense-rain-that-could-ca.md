Severe weather warnings are in effect across Greater Manchester this weekend, forecasting heavy rain and strong winds. A yellow warning is specifically for the region, affecting areas like Bolton, Bury, Oldham, Rochdale, Tameside, Manchester, Salford, and Wigan. Rain is set to hit from 6pm on Saturday until 6pm on Monday, with potential flooding and strong winds expected. The Met Office warns that the weather will begin dry but will turn wet and windy, with the possibility of patchy rain in Greater Manchester by tonight. Sunday will see continued unsettled conditions, primarily impacting Cumbria, which has an amber warning due to forecasted heavy rainfall exceeding 200mm. The Environment Agency highlights risks of surface water flooding and advises against driving through floodwater. Residents are encouraged to check their flood risk and stay updated via @EnvAgency on X.
