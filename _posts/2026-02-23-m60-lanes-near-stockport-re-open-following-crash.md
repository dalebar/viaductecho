The M60 near Stockport has reopened after a crash this morning, February 23. The incident occurred just after 7am, leading to two lanes being closed between Portwood Roundabout and Stockport Pyramid, causing significant delays that stretched to surrounding areas like Reddish and Ashton. Even though the lanes have reopened, some delays are still reported as of 9.27am.
