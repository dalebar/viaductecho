A customer from a social supermarket in Reddish is chuffed to be able to afford a few extra treats for Christmas, thanks to the savings on her weekly groceries. The supermarket, which opened in June 2024, aims to provide low-cost, quality food for locals facing financial challenges. With a focus on fresh fruit, veggies, and frozen goods, it helps folks whip up meals on a budget. Mandy Carter, another Reddish resident, shared how the weekly selection varies, often featuring staples like potatoes and onions, perfect for making hearty soups.
