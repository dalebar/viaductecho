Rochdale's 'Stories We Tell' immersive tour has become the town's hottest ticket, offering a unique blend of culture, music, and local history. The Manchester Evening News joined the well-attended event, where participants donned illuminated headphones that transported them through time with vivid soundscapes of the bustling Milkstone Road and more. Guided by locals, including Hannah Thomas-Byrne, the tour highlighted key spots like St John The Baptist Church and The Regal Moon, a former cinema turned pub. The experience culminated at the stunning Rochdale Town Hall, showcasing the town's rich heritage and community spirit. With a focus on uplifting local narratives, the tour was backed by the Breaking Barriers project and involved the Young Company, providing young artists with opportunities to shine. Attendees emerged with renewed pride in Rochdale, eager to counter negative stereotypes and celebrate their vibrant identity.
