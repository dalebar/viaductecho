There have been numerous flight delays at Manchester Airport this morning due to heavy rain, with a yellow weather warning in effect until midnight. Various airlines including British Airways, Ryanair, and easyJet have been impacted, with flights to destinations like Copenhagen, Paris, and Malaga affected. Passengers are encouraged to keep an eye on flight statuses as delays can stem from weather, staffing, or issues at the origin airport. As of 10:45am, several flights are experiencing delays of over 25 minutes, including Norwegian to Copenhagen and easyJet to Lisbon. If you're flying today, best to check with your airline frequently for updates.
