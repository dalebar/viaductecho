Police are looking for 15-year-old Layton Beattie, who’s been missing from his home in West Kirkby, Merseyside, for over 24 hours. He was last seen at 2:10 PM on December 12. Layton is around 6ft tall, slim build, with short brown hair and a pale complexion. He was wearing a blue tracksuit, a blue puffer jacket, and blue Nike Air Max trainers. It's noted that he frequently visits Birkenhead, Liverpool city centre, and Manchester. The police have shared a description and photo to aid in locating him. They’re asking anyone with any info or sightings of Layton to get in touch via their contact form or by calling 101.
