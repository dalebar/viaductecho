A teenager, Bradley Price, 19, from Middleton, has appeared in court following a collision where a mum with a pram was injured by his e-scooter. The incident occurred on Rochdale Road, and although the mum suffered minor injuries, her baby was unharmed. Price reportedly needed treatment for bleeding. He faces several charges, including possession of an offensive weapon, driving without insurance, dangerous driving, and causing serious injury by dangerous driving. The court proceedings are the latest from this unfortunate incident.
