Police are investigating a shooting at a home on School House Road in Oldham, where gunfire was reported on Sunday, February 15. Officers arrived around 1pm after reports of criminal damage, only to find multiple bullet holes in the front window. Thankfully, no injuries were reported. The police have cordoned off the area as they continue their investigation, with forensic teams examining the scene. Neighbours noted that the street had previously experienced issues but had been calm lately. Authorities are urging anyone with information, including CCTV or dashcam footage, to get in touch.
