This weekend in Stockport, there’s plenty going on, all thanks to sponsors like EatGoodWest UK and Waterside Hotel & Leisure Club. The Waterside Hotel is offering a delightful Christmas lunch menu from 27 November until 19 December for £34.95, featuring festive dishes like turkey and a vegan Wellington. Also, on Sunday 14 December, they’ll host a Sunday Social Carvery from 12-6pm at £16.95 for adults. 

For some festive cheer, head to St Mary’s Church on Friday 5 December for a Christmas Worship Night, or catch the amateur production of "Jeeves and Wooster in Perfect Nonsense" at Chads Theatre from 6-7 December. Don’t miss the Joy & Positivity Christmas Tree Festival at Signol Community Centre, with crafts and activities supporting women's wellbeing.

Lastly, futureshock wrestling returns to Stockport Masonic Guildhall on 7 December, and you can look forward to the panto "Aladdin" at Stockport Plaza from December 5, 2025. Keep an eye on the What's On section for more events!
