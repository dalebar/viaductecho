Thirteen suspects have been charged following a major police operation across Greater Manchester on February 18, targeting organised crime. Numerous raids were conducted at 22 locations, resulting in 23 arrests of men and boys aged 13 to 55. Charges include serious offences like conspiracy to supply cocaine, heroin, and human trafficking. Notable suspects include members of the Azad family, including Kuba (23), Mohammed (56), Kovan (29), and Kueka (26), all facing multiple drug-related charges. Others charged include Karl McDonagh, Saman Dousti, Hedi Garmseri, Anwar Shaikha, Dominic Mather, Ali Ahmed, Reberash Rach, Reiber Rashid, and Dyar Ahmed. They'll appear at Manchester Magistrates' Court on February 20, while ten others have been released on bail.
