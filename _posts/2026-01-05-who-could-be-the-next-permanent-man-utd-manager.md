Manchester United is on the hunt for a new manager after Erik ten Hag, Ralf Rangnick, and Amorim didn't deliver. There's a debate about whether they should go for someone with proven Premier League experience or consider an internal choice. Big names across Europe are potential targets, but Gareth Southgate has distanced himself from the role, stating he won't return to coaching for at least a year post-England.
