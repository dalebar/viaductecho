Manchester Airport, the UK’s third-busiest, has seen a big change with the upcoming full opening of the new Terminal 2. This revamped terminal will boast a variety of cafes, bars, and amenities for travellers, whether popping over to Ireland or jetting off to the Far East. The £1.3 billion project has prompted a major reorganisation of airlines across the terminals. Regular flyers might be in the know about their terminals, but holidaymakers should definitely double-check where they’re headed. A handy list of every airline operating at Manchester Airport, sorted alphabetically by terminal, is available for those needing clarity.
