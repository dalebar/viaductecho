Police in Manchester are urgently looking for a missing man named Scott, last seen at North Manchester General Hospital on Sunday, December 14. They’re increasingly concerned for the 48-year-old’s safety. Scott, from Newton Heath, is about 5ft 10in, slim build, with a bald head. He was wearing a black Trailberg bubble coat, navy joggers, and black Air Max trainers. If anyone has seen him or knows where he might be, they’re urged to contact Greater Manchester Police at 0161 856 7821 or 101, quoting log 2363 of 14/12/25.
