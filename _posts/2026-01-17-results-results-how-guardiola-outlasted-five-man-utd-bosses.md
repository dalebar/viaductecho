As Pep Guardiola heads into his sixth Manchester derby as City’s manager, he's facing his sixth different United boss in nearly a decade. Over the last ten years, United have had a revolving door of managers, including José Mourinho, Ole Gunnar Solskjær, Ralf Rangnick, Erik ten Hag, and now Amorim. Guardiola, already City’s most successful manager, is cementing his legacy as one of the greatest ever, thanks to his trophy-laden spells at Barcelona and Bayern Munich. Meanwhile, United have consistently struggled to match City’s performance, finishing as close as second under Guardiola but never managing to surpass their rivals in the league.
