A coroner's report indicates that further tests are required to determine the cause of comedian Justin Moorhouse's son's death. Greater Manchester Police have investigated the scene, finding no signs of foul play or third-party involvement. Justin, who is well-known for his roles in Phoenix Nights, Coronation Street, and Looking for Eric, shared the sad news of his son's passing on social media.
