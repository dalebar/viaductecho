High Peak Comps is running some fab prize draws this Christmas, including their biggest ever giveaway – a whopping £100,000! Tickets are just 99p each, and you can grab up to 3,000. The draw ends on Christmas Day, with the winner announced live on their Facebook page at 1pm. They’re also offering a chance to win a year's free rent or mortgage payments for 2026, with tickets at just 10p each, closing on 31st December and a draw at 8pm.

High Peak Comps, which launched in March 2020, aims to provide fair chances to win amazing prizes and has already given away £10m. They’re also big on supporting local causes, recently donating a microwave to Stepping Hill Hospital. Plus, loads of folks have been winning daily, like Hannah who snagged £1,000! For more info, check out their Facebook page or drop them an email if you’ve got a community project that needs support.
