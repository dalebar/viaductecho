This weekend in Greater Manchester, Saturday is looking decent with sunny intervals and highs of 9°C, though it’ll cool down to 7°C in the evening. Sunday shifts to a cloudy vibe, climbing up to 10°C by the afternoon but expect a fair chance of rain later on. Saturday will have some nice prolonged sunny spells in the North West, but things will turn cloudier as the day goes on. Sunday will remain mostly dull with occasional drizzle. Winds are light overall. Looking ahead to next week, it's set to be generally cloudy with temperatures peaking at 10°C and dipping to 4°C on Christmas Day.
