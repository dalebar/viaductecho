The Christmas markets are a hit, bringing millions together with their festive wooden chalet bars and bratwurst stalls, even if the prices are a bit steep. Although there's been some grumbling about costs, many, like 20-year-olds Leo and Amy, find the experience worth it—spending £22 on Yorkshire pudding wraps. Hayley, 40, from Littleborough, even thinks the prices are surprisingly reasonable. Stall owner Isabelle loves the bustling atmosphere, and it’s clear the markets spark joy in the community.
