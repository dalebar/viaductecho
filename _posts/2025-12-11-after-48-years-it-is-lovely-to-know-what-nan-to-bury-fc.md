Bury FC has honoured Ms Curtis, a beloved figure associated with the club for nearly 50 years, following her passing. Her connection to Bury FC started with her father, Bill Gorman, a pre-war player who later moved to Brentford. The club released a heartfelt tribute on their website, celebrating her lifelong dedication. Ms Kent mentioned that the family is hopeful for a tribute during the upcoming home match against Mossley on 13 December.
