Manchester's dining scene is thriving, boasting a range of restaurants from upscale Michelin-star spots like Mana and Skof to casual curry cafes. The city has transformed from A.A. Gill’s former critique to a culinary hotspot celebrating diverse cuisines. Top picks include:

- **Erst**: A stylish wine bar in Ancoats, known for its natural wines and small plates.
- **Climat**: A rooftop venue with stunning views and a refined menu.
- **Sparrows**: Celebrated for its Central European dishes, especially dumplings.
- **Skof**: A Michelin-starred restaurant offering an ambitious tasting menu in NOMA.
- **Bangkok’s Diner's Club**: Authentic Thai dishes served with a smoky twist in Ancoats.

Other notable mentions are Mi & Pho for delightful Vietnamese, Dishoom for a taste of Bombay, and Marhaba for exceptional Pakistani home-cooking. Each restaurant offers unique dishes and vibes, showcasing Manchester’s vibrant food culture. Whether you're after fine dining or comfort food, there's plenty to explore in this bustling metropolis.
