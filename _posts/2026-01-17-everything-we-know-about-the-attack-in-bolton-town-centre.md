In the early hours, an assault in Bolton town centre left blood splattered on the pavement. Police were called to Bradshawgate around 5:50am, finding an 18-year-old man with serious head injuries, who was then taken to hospital. A section of the area was cordoned off, with officers and forensic teams gathering evidence. A 17-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of assault related to the incident, and investigations are still ongoing. The victim remains in hospital as police look into the case further.
