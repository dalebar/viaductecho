Fulham has snapped up Norwegian winger Oscar Bobb from Manchester City for £27 million. The lad, who's come through City's academy, has racked up 47 appearances for the first team, netting three goals and providing four assists. However, since Antoine Semenyo's move to City in January, Bobb's had a tougher time getting game time.
