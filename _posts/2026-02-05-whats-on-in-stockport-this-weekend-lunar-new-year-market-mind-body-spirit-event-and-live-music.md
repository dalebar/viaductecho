This weekend in Stockport is buzzing with events! Sponsored by EatGoodWest UK and Waterside Hotel & Leisure Club, you can enjoy the Lunar New Year Festival at Merseyway Shopping Centre, featuring mouth-watering Asian street food and live performances from 9am to 5pm on Saturday and Sunday. 

At the Stockport Masonic Guildhall, there's a Mind Body Spirit event from 10am to 6pm, offering over 65 exhibitors, workshops, and free talks on various wellness topics, including healing techniques and psychic experiences. 

For families, St Andrew's Church in Cheadle Hulme is hosting a concert from 3-5pm, showcasing delightful music including Lenny Sayers’ nonsense poems and a fun story about Tubby the tuba. Tickets are only £12 for adults, with kids and carers getting in free.

Make the most of your weekend and check out these fab events in Stockport!
