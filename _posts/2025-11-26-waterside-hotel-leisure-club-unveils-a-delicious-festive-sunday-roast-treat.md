Waterside Hotel & Leisure Club is hosting a one-day-only Sunday Social Carvery on 14th December from 12pm to 6pm. Guests can enjoy a traditional roast carvery with all the trimmings, including unlimited vegetables and gravy, served in a family-friendly atmosphere. No booking is necessary—just turn up and dig in! Pricing is £16.95 for adults and £6.95 for children, with little ones under 3 eating free. This event is aimed at bringing people together for a warm festive experience. For more details on dining and future events, check out their website.
