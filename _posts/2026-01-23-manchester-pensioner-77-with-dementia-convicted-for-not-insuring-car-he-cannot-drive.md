A 77-year-old man from Middleton, Greater Manchester, has been convicted for not insuring his car after being diagnosed with severe dementia and stopping driving in December 2024. He faced prosecution in a fast-track court after the DVLA fined him for the uninsured vehicle in August 2025, but he had no memory of receiving the fine.
