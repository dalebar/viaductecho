Manchester City are on the verge of sealing a £65m deal with Bournemouth for Antoine Semenyo. However, Bournemouth's manager, Andoni Iraola, firmly states that Semenyo hasn’t played his last game for the club yet. Despite conceding a penalty in a recent draw against Chelsea, Semenyo demonstrated his value by contributing two long throw-ins that helped secure both goals for Bournemouth.
