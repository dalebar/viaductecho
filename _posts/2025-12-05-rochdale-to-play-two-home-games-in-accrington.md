Rochdale, topping the National League, will host two home matches at Accrington Stanley's Wham Stadium while their own pitch gets a bit of TLC. They're currently ahead of Carlisle United on goal difference but have a couple of games in hand thanks to earlier postponements caused by a waterlogged pitch.
