A young man named Drew McCormick has been identified as the victim in a murder investigation in Wythenshawe. He was stabbed late on February 5, found seriously injured on Southwick Road, and sadly died at the scene. Friends have described him as the "life and soul of the party," highlighting his caring and funny nature. Many shared heartfelt tributes, expressing shock at his passing and celebrating his vibrant character. 

Police have arrested a 19-year-old man on suspicion of murder, who remains in custody as investigations continue. Forensic teams are at the scene, collecting evidence, while residents in the area expressed their disbelief and concern over such violence in their neighbourhood. Authorities are urging anyone with information to come forward, whether by contacting Greater Manchester Police or the anonymous charity Crimestoppers.
