Today in Manchester, several planes were spotted flying in formation over Bury and the city centre, sparking curiosity among plane spotters. A video captured six aircraft flying above Oxford Street around 4pm, drawing attention for being quite unusual. Enthusiasts on social media, including @RossPartin34657 and David Hartley, shared photos and commented on the sighting, speculating whether they belonged to the RAF or USAF. Hartley noted it was a first for him to see planes flying side by side. The Manchester Evening News has reached out to the Ministry of Defence for further insight.
