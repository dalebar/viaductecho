A woman's body has been discovered in the Bridgewater Canal in Altrincham. Police were alerted on the morning of December 10, 2025, after reports of a body in the water near Atlantic Street. Emergency services responded, and part of the canal path was closed off for a thorough search by a specialist police team. Greater Manchester Police confirmed the tragic event and are working to identify the woman to support her loved ones. They stated they would provide updates on the circumstances surrounding her death as the investigation continues.
