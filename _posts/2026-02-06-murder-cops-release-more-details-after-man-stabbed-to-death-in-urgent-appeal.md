A man in his 20s, named Drew McCormick by friends, was fatally stabbed during a dispute on Southwick Road in Northern Moor, Manchester, on Thursday, February 5. Police responded to reports of serious injuries and found him at the scene, where he sadly died. A 19-year-old man is in custody on suspicion of murder, with indications that both men knew each other. Witnesses described a chaotic scene, including a woman pleading during the incident. Greater Manchester Police are leading the investigation and have appealed for information, particularly from anyone with dashcam or CCTV footage. Tributes poured in from friends, who remembered him as a vibrant character who brought joy to those around him. Police forensics are currently at the scene, and a cordon remains in place as investigations continue. Anyone with information is urged to contact GMP or Crimestoppers.
