The Town Street Fryer in Marple Bridge has been recognised as one of the UK's top 50 fish and chip takeaway spots by the 2026 Fry Awards. This accolade, organised by Fry Magazine, highlights the achievements of fish and chip shops nationwide, judged by mystery diners who assess the food, cleanliness, service, and value for money. The Fryer scored an impressive 97% or higher to secure its spot on the list. The team expressed their excitement on social media, thanking their staff and customers for their ongoing support. For more details, you can check out their Facebook page.
