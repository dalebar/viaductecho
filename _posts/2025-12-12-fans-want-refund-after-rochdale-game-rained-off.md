Southend United fans are asking for a refund after their away match against Rochdale, which involved a 250-mile trip, got cancelled for the second time due to heavy rain. The players were pulled off the pitch as conditions worsened during the game. The Shrimpers Trust pointed out that the pitch quality was already questionable before kick-off because of the weather.
