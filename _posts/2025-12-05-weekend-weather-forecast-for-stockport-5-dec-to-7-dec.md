Stockport's weekend weather forecast shows a mix of clouds and rain with humid conditions throughout. 

**Friday:** Expect persistent rain and cloudy skies, with temperatures reaching 9°C and dipping to 1°C. It’ll feel quite chilly due to humidity at 85% and strong winds up to 36 mph.

**Saturday:** A bit of a break with partly cloudy skies but still a high chance of rain. Temperatures will be milder at 11°C max and 8°C min, with humidity at 88% and winds calming to 22 mph.

**Sunday:** Cloudy and more rain showers return, with highs of 11°C and lows of 6°C. Humidity peaks at 91%, and while the chance of rain drops slightly to 84%, it's still a good idea to have an umbrella at hand.

**Commute Advice:** Drive safely in slippery conditions and allow extra travel time. 

**Weekend Plans:** Consider indoor activities, but if outdoors, pack waterproof gear and find shelter during downpours.
