Residents evacuated after the recent terror attack at Heaton Park synagogue are still waiting to return home. Heather, living nearby, shared her frustrations about the lack of information from authorities on when they'll be able to go back. The attack, which occurred on October 2, resulted in two deaths and several injuries. The attacker was shot by police shortly after the incident. Many evacuees, including families and those caring for disabled individuals, remain at the Abraham Moss Centre, unsure about their futures.
