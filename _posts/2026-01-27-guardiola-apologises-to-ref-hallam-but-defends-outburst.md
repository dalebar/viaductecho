Pep Guardiola has apologised to new Premier League referee Farai Hallam for any offence caused after expressing his frustration over officiating following Manchester City's win against Wolves. While he acknowledges that referees support each other, Guardiola's criticisms reflect a growing discontent with refereeing decisions this season. Looking ahead, City faces a crucial European match against Galatasaray, needing a win to improve their chances of finishing in the top eight and avoiding extra play-off games.
