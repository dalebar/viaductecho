Tributes have poured in for Stephen Cromarty, a beloved dad and Rochdale AFC supporter, whose body was found in Salford Quays on December 5. His passing has deeply affected friends, family, and the Rochdale AFC community. His daughter shared a heartfelt tribute on X, saying, “Forever in my heart. I’ll miss you, Dad.” The club responded with condolences, highlighting Mr Cromarty's passion for football, which influenced his daughter's fandom. Many others shared memories of his kindness and his infectious enthusiasm at matches. Fans described him as a “great bloke” and “one in a million,” noting how he often rallied supporters during games. The Rochdale fan forum also expressed their sadness, stating, “Awful news this morning,” and sending love to his family. An inquest will be conducted, and the community continues to remember Mr Cromarty fondly during this difficult time.
