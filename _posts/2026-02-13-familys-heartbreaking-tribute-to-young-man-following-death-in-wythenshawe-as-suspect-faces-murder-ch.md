Drew McCormick, a 29-year-old from Wythenshawe, tragically passed away following an incident on February 5. His family is heartbroken, describing him as a "fun-loving person" with a bright smile and a huge heart. They've expressed that there are no words to capture the impact he had on their lives and that he'll forever be in their hearts. Friends have shared fond memories, highlighting his vibrant personality and ability to bring joy to others. Lewis Morley, 19, has been charged with Drew's murder and is set to appear in court on March 10. A crowdfunding page has been established to assist with funeral expenses, while Greater Manchester Police's Major Incident Team continues its investigation. They're urging anyone with information or footage to come forward.
