Two men were hospitalised after a serious assault in East Manchester's Clayton on December 9, around 7pm. Police found them with serious, but thankfully not life-threatening injuries. Both men, aged 29 and 24, were arrested on suspicion of being involved in drug supply. Authorities have declared this a targeted attack, posing no danger to the wider public. Greater Manchester Police are urging anyone with relevant information or footage to come forward, whether it's CCTV or dashcam. People can report info by calling 101 and quoting log number 3100 from December 9, or through GMP's LiveChat on their website. Alternatively, reports can be made to Crimestoppers at 0800 555 111.
