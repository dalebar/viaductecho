A serious crash occurred this morning at the busy junction of Hollyhedge Road and Brownley Road in Wythenshawe, south Manchester. Emergency services, including police, firefighters, and paramedics, responded quickly, blocking off part of the road for safety. Witness Tracy Chapman described hearing a loud bang before seeing two damaged cars—a black Volkswagen Golf and a black Mercedes-Benz SUV—along with a Bee Network bus involved in the incident. Fire crews were spotted examining the Mercedes. Traffic build-up is reported in the area. The crash took place around 8.15am, and updates are awaited as emergency services continue to work on the scene.
