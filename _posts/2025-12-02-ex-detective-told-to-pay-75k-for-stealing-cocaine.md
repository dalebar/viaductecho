A detective who stole nearly £400,000 worth of cocaine from police storage has been sentenced to repay over £75,000, or he’ll face additional jail time. Judge Neil Flewitt KC highlighted the betrayal of trust he displayed towards both his colleagues and the community. Helen Hughes from the Crown Prosecution Service emphasised that the public rightfully expects police officers to follow the law.
