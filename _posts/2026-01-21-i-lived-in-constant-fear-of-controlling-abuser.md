A victim of James Saxon shared her harrowing experience in court, detailing the fear and injury she endured from his abusive behaviour. She described the physical pain from assaults and the deeper emotional scars left behind. Court evidence included doorbell footage showing Saxon verbally abusing her and others. The victim highlighted how his controlling actions stripped her of her confidence, safety, independence, and peace of mind. After the sentencing, PC Elizabeth Andrews emphasised the severe impact of such coercive behaviour and encouraged others experiencing similar situations to come forward.
