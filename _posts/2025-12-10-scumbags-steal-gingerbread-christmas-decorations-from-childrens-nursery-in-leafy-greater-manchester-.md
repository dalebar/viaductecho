The festive cheer took a hit at The Heatons Preschool and Day Nursery in Heaton Moor when their stunning Christmas display, including two life-size gingerbread men, went missing. The nursery staff crafted an enchanting display with baubles and bows, and the gingerbread figures were a highlight for the children. Sadly, when staff arrived one morning, the decorations had vanished. They made a heartfelt plea on Facebook, expressing their dismay and asking the community for help in locating the stolen gingerbread men. Comments flooded in from shocked locals expressing their disappointment and anger over the theft, with many hoping for the return of the beloved decorations. The nursery is reviewing CCTV footage for clues and is keen to see their festive magic restored. If you happen to spot the gingerbread duo, they encourage you to reach out to them or local news.
