Greater Manchester Police are searching for 36-year-old Nicholas Mullen, who is wanted on recall to prison. Mullen has connections to Manchester, Wigan, and Salford. The police made an appeal on their Wigan and Leigh Facebook page, urging anyone with information about his whereabouts to reach out. They provided contact details for reporting tips, either through police or anonymously via Crimestoppers.
