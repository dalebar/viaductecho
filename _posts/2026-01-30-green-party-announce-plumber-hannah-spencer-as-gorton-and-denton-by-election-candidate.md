The Green Party has named Hannah Spencer as their candidate for the Gorton and Denton by-election. A plumber and current Trafford councillor, Spencer, 34, has lived in Greater Manchester her whole life. She previously ran in the 2024 Greater Manchester mayoral election, finishing fifth. Spencer emphasised the importance of the by-election, presenting it as a choice between hope with the Greens and division with Reform.
