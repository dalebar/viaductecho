Emergency services are tackling a serious incident on the Crewe to Manchester railway line this morning, with all lines between Crewe and Stockport closed. Services from Avanti West Coast, Northern, and Transport for Wales are experiencing delays or cancellations. National Rail confirmed that the emergency services are on the scene, and trains may be delayed by up to 60 minutes or revised. Crewe Nub News is in touch with Cheshire Police for more details.
