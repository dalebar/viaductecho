Residents in Urmston, Trafford, are in shock after a shooting on Friday night that left a 21-year-old man seriously injured. The incident occurred around 8:40pm, prompting a police cordon on Higher Road and a response from armed police. The victim remains in hospital, stable but critical. Local businesses and residents expressed their disbelief, noting that such violent incidents are rare in the area. Edip Berkul, who has lived in Urmston for 16 years, voiced concerns about safety, saying the incident was shocking for such a close-knit community. Another business owner mentioned the potential impact on trade due to fears following the shooting. Chief Inspector Darren Lomas reassured the public that the attack appeared targeted with no wider threat, and increased police patrols will continue. Authorities are asking anyone with information or footage of the incident to come forward.
