Mason Mount hasn't played a full 90 minutes in the Premier League for ages. At United, he's only managed to stay on the pitch for over 80 minutes once, which was during a tough 4-0 defeat to Palace in May 2024. The second half saw Palace lose some of their spark, likely due to their hectic schedule and Ismaila Sarr's injury in the 38th minute.
