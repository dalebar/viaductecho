Dough Club, a trendy halal pizza place from Withington, Manchester, is making waves across the globe, participating in the Qatar International Food Festival in Doha, 4,000 miles away from home. Since opening in September 2024, it’s gained a loyal following for its delicious Detroit-style pizzas with a thick, chewy crust and a variety of vibrant toppings, earning rave reviews from local food critics. Following its initial success, Dough Club has expanded to two more locations in Greater Manchester. At the festival, the restaurant is reportedly bustling, with customers queuing up as staff work diligently to prepare orders, even selling out at times. It’s clear that Dough Club is on the rise, bringing a slice of Manchester's culinary scene to an international audience!
