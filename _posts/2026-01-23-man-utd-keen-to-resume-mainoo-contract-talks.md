Manchester United are looking to restart discussions with midfielder Kobbie Mainoo about a new contract that would align his pay with that of senior squad members. The 20-year-old has been getting his chance on the pitch, featuring in the FA Cup loss to Brighton and retaining his spot for the recent derby win against Manchester City under Michael Carrick's management.
