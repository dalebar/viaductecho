A woman tragically lost her life after being struck by a car on Bury New Road in a serious accident on Thursday night (February 5). Emergency services rushed to the scene near Prestwich, where the road was sealed off for investigations by the Serious Collision Unit. The victim, whose age hasn't been disclosed, was declared dead at the spot. A suspect has been arrested on suspicion of causing death by dangerous driving and is in custody for questioning. Photos from the scene reveal a significant police presence and multiple ambulances, along with a blue forensic tent set up as part of the ongoing investigation. Greater Manchester Police are expected to provide a full statement soon.
