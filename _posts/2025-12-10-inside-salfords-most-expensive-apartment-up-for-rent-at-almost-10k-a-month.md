A posh penthouse in Salford is now on the market for rent at a whopping £9,925 monthly, making it the most expensive apartment in the area. Located atop the Cortland At Colliers Yard complex, it features three double bedrooms, three bathrooms, and a spacious open-plan living area with stunning views of Manchester. The flat is modern, fully furnished, and pet-friendly, ready for move-in from December 29. The complex offers fantastic amenities, including a gym, spin studio, games room, private dining areas, and co-working spaces. Savills highlights the high-quality furnishings and well-equipped kitchen, plus all residents benefit from fast WiFi, 24/7 support, and two parking spaces. With extensive wellness facilities and beautifully designed communal areas, it's perfect for those who enjoy a touch of luxury in their living space, especially with quiet work areas for remote workers.
