Pavers, the family-owned footwear retailer, is opening a new shop in Broadstone Mill, Reddish, on Thursday 26 February. The store will feature a wide selection of stylish and comfy footwear for men and women, including boots and casual shoes, plus accessories and everyday essentials. To celebrate the launch, Pavers is hosting a monthly shoe giveaway for the next year, with customers able to enter the ballot during their visit. Sunil Malhotra, Area Manager, expressed his excitement about joining the Mill and prioritising customer satisfaction. Broadstone Mill staff also shared their enthusiasm, noting that Pavers is a perfect fit for their commitment to quality and value.
