Pep Guardiola mentioned that Manchester City needs to start planning for his eventual departure, acknowledging that he won't be around forever. As the search for his successor kicks off, sporting director Hugo Viana is expected to take the lead. The challenge for the next manager is steep, given Guardiola's remarkable success. There are numerous high-profile candidates in Europe, particularly from Spain, but there's also the question of whether City might promote from within. It's all a bit up in the air at the moment!
