A person has sustained serious injuries from a shooting in Urmston, Trafford, Greater Manchester. The incident occurred on Higher Road at around 8.42pm on January 16. Paramedics treated the victim at the scene, and armed police were also on site. A cordon was established near Urmston railway station, with a ripped puffa jacket found within the secured area. Greater Manchester Police have upped patrols in the vicinity following the event, and enquiries are still ongoing, with further details yet to be confirmed. Authorities are calling for witnesses to come forward.
