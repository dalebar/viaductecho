A brawl erupted between fans at the Manchester City vs Leeds match on November 29, leading to a police investigation. Five people were arrested for affray during the incident at the Etihad Stadium. While some City fans claimed they were attacked by Leeds supporters, the official Manchester City club account clarified that Leeds fans were not involved and that the injuries reported were exaggerated. They stated that the situation was less severe than the social media rumours suggested. Greater Manchester Police confirmed their ongoing inquiry, with all five arrested individuals currently on bail. On the pitch, City secured a 3-2 win thanks to a last-minute goal from Phil Foden.
