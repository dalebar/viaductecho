Stockport County's manager, Dave Challinor, aims to surpass last season's points total. The team has seen a decent form lately, winning six of their last 11 league matches and remaining unbeaten in their last five games across all competitions. This streak even includes a solid 1-1 draw against league leaders Cardiff on Saturday.
