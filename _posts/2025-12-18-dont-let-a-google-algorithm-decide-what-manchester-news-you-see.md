Growing up with the Manchester Evening News (MEN) means having a local voice that tells our city's stories with heart. For 157 years, the MEN has reported on everything from wars to celebrations, always prioritising the community. However, the way people access news has changed, with algorithms sometimes burying local reporting. A recent attack on a Manchester synagogue showed that while MEN had a full team reporting live, it got pushed down Google search results, overshadowed by generic sources.
