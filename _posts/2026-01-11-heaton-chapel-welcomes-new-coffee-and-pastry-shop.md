Briola, a new coffee and pastry shop, is set to open in Heaton Chapel on 10 January at the former Belcasa Concept site. Soreen Billbas, the founder, envisions it as a community hub for locals to relax and enjoy upscale, affordable eats. Following a successful trial run on 20 December, the menu focuses on high-quality, fresh offerings with natural ingredients, featuring hand-picked arabica coffee, focaccia sandwiches, and an array of pastries, including unique treats from the caf√©'s diverse team. There are plans for community events and classes in the future, with potential menu expansions based on customer feedback. Expect opening hours of 8am-4pm Tuesday to Friday, and 10am-4pm on weekends. You can check out Briola at 395 Manchester Rd, Stockport SK4 5B.
