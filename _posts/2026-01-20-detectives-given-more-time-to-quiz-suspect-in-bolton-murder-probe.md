Detectives in Bolton are continuing their murder investigation after a woman was found dead in a flat on Kempton Gardens. Emergency services were called on January 19 when concerns for her welfare were raised, but sadly, she could not be saved. A 33-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and investigators have since been granted more time to question him. Forensic teams were extensively working at the scene, with a police cordon in place to secure the area.
