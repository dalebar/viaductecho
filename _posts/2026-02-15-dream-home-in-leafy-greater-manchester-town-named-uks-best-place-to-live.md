When hunting for a new pad, don’t forget about location—it matters just as much as the property! Marple, a lovely town in Stockport, has just been crowned the UK’s 'best place to live' by Garrington Property Finder. Known for its charm and community vibe, Marple has a top property listed at £799,999. The five-bed home on Buxton Lane is a spacious delight, featuring a modern design with a stunning open-plan kitchen and lovely garden. With an average house price of £517,119, buyers are drawn to Marple for a better lifestyle, despite it being above the national average. The town is surrounded by beautiful countryside, has easy train links to Manchester, and a vibrant centre with cafes and a cinema. Plus, it hosts regular community events and boasts excellent schools. Marple really could be the perfect mix of peaceful living and city access!
