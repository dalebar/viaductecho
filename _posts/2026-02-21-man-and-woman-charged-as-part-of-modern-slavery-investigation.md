A couple from Salford, Nicolae Branovici, 33, and Maria Branovici, 26, have been charged with modern slavery as part of a Greater Manchester Police investigation. Nicolae faces charges for modern slavery and cocaine possession, while Maria is charged solely with modern slavery. They were arrested on February 19, 2026. GMP has shared this news on their Facebook page and encourages anyone with concerns about potential victims of exploitation to reach out to the Modern Slavery Helpline.
