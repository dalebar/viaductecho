The latest data from the Land Registry shows a big difference in house prices across Greater Manchester. Little Hulton in Salford is the cheapest spot, with homes averaging £178,959, followed closely by Clayton at £182,536. Other affordable areas include Bolton, Ordsall, and Bardsley, with prices ranging from about £183,000 to £192,000. 

On the pricey end, Altrincham leads the pack with an average house price of £596,067, followed by another part of Altrincham at £519,997. Bramhall, Chorlton, and Didsbury are also on the higher side, with prices peaking around £432,000 and above. 

So whether you're looking for a bargain or fancy a more upscale area, Greater Manchester certainly has it all! Check out the interactive map for average prices in specific postcodes.
