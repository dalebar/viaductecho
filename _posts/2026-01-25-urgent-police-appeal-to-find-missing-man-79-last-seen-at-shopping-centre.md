Police are urgently seeking help to locate a missing 79-year-old pensioner named James, last seen at Mill Gate shopping centre in Bury on Saturday afternoon around 4pm. Greater Manchester Police have grown increasingly concerned for his welfare, sharing his image on social media. James is from Bolton, stands at 5ft 10ins tall, and was last spotted wearing light-coloured trousers and a grey hooded jacket with red and white trim. If you have any information, please contact police on 101 and quote log number 2512 from 24/01/2026.
