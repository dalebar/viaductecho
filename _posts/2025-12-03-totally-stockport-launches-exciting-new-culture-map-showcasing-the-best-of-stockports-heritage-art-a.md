Totally Stockport has launched a new Culture Map, celebrating the town's heritage, art, and green spaces. This vibrant guide showcases everything from historical landmarks and street art to filming locations and lovely parks, making it a great resource for both locals and newcomers. You can grab a free copy from local businesses or download it from the Totally Stockport website. Shaun Freeman-Jones, the BID Manager, highlights how the map reflects Stockport's unique charm and hopes it encourages exploration of the town. It's designed for anyone looking for a cultural day out or those snapping photos. So, pick up your map and uncover the wonderful stories hidden around Stockport!
