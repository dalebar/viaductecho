A new Card Factory has just opened in Cheadle Hulme at Unit 24 in the precinct. It offers a wide variety of greetings cards, balloons, party supplies, and gift wrap, perfect for all your special occasions. The store is open Monday to Saturday from 9am to 5.30pm, and on Sundays from 10am to 4pm. A spokesperson mentioned their commitment to helping customers make memorable moments, whether for a birthday, anniversary, or other celebrations.
