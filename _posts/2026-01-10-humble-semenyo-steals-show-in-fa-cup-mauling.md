Antoine Semenyo kicked off his Manchester City journey in style, following a stunning send-off at Bournemouth. Just four days in, he played a key role in a record-equalling 9-0 victory for City. The lad was absolutely beaming at full-time, matching the stadium's bright lights. The impressive 17-year-old debutant Ryan McAidoo rounded off the scoring just before the end, sparking chants from the fans for one more goal.
