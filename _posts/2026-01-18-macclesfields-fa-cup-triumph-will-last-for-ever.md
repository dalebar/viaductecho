Macclesfield fans are still in shock after their part-time team pulled off a stunning upset by beating FA Cup holders Crystal Palace 2-1 in the third round. Long-time supporter Jonathan Smart, 62, is among those trying to wrap his head around the historic win. Match day secretary Janey Parish caught the Macclesfield spirit through her daughter's involvement in the women's team. Local chip shop owner Eric Meta, originally a Man Utd fan, expressed pride in his adopted town's success. Fan-turned-director Brigg Simpson called the day "surreal" as he reflected on the remarkable match.
