Police are asking for help to find 16-year-old Coby, who's been missing from Leigh since February 17. He was last seen at 10am on Wordsworth Avenue and has links to Salford. Coby is described as a white male, about 5ft 10, very slim, with brown hair typically styled in a skin fade. Greater Manchester Police (GMP) is worried about his safety and has released a photo to aid in the search. They urge anyone with information to get in touch, using 0161 856 7102, 101, or 999 in emergencies, quoting log number 2344-190226.
