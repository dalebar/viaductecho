One lane of the M62 westbound is closed due to flooding between Junctions 20 and 19, with the issue first reported around 10pm on Tuesday. Traffic data company Inrix noted that flooding is causing the blockage, impacting drivers from Rochdale to Heywood. The Met Office has also issued a yellow weather warning for ice, valid until 10am on Monday, cautioning that icy patches could form overnight, potentially causing disruptions, especially on Wednesday morning. So, keep an eye out for those tricky conditions!
