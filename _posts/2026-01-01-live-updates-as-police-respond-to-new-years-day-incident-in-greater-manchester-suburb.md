Greater Manchester Police are currently responding to a major incident on Tarn Drive in Bury after residents reported significant police activity early on New Year’s Day. Witnesses mentioned police arriving around 2:30am, with the area cordoned off by 12:30am. One resident noted they woke up to find a police presence at 6am, while another described a crime scene tent at a property’s door and forensics specialists on-site. The local community is understandably concerned, with some expressing shock at the situation. The police have been contacted for further information as updates are expected.
