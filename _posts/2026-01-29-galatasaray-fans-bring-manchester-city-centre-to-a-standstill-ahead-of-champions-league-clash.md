Parts of Manchester city centre are buzzing ahead of Manchester City's Champions League match against Galatasaray. Thousands of Turkish fans have gathered at Shambles Square, chanting and celebrating before their escorted walk to the Etihad Stadium. The atmosphere is lively, with red flares lighting up the streets and fans dancing. Police are present, but there are no reports of any disturbances. 

The first fans have begun arriving at the stadium, which kicks off at 8pm tonight. Videos shared on social media show the vibrant march, with fans setting off fireworks and creating a festive scene. A few photographers captured stunning moments of the fans celebrating. Overall, it's a colourful pre-match spectacle, showcasing the passionate support for Galatasaray in the heart of Manchester.
