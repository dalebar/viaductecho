A bunch of motorway closures are on the cards around Greater Manchester and a bit beyond, starting Monday, February 9. The M60, M56, M62, M6, and M61 will all see some action during the night. Key closures include:

- M6 Southbound at Knutsford Services (entry/exit) from 21:00 to 06:00
- M60 Anti-clockwise at Junction 5 (entry slip) from 21:00 to 06:00
- M61 Southbound at A666/M61 link from 21:00 to 06:00
- M56 Westbound from M56 to M6 link from 21:00 to 06:00

Expect various closures on entries/exits and carriageways throughout the night, many closing between 21:00 and 06:00. Just make sure to check ahead if you're planning to hit the road, and stay safe out there!
