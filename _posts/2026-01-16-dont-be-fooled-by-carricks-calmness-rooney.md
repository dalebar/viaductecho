Wayne Rooney believes Michael Carrick will "steady the ship" at Manchester United, but he warns fans not to be misled by Carrick's laid-back manner. While Carrick's role is temporary, it raises questions about who the board will choose for a permanent position in the summer. Currently, United are in seventh place in the Premier League, having won just one of their last six matches. Carrick has bolstered his coaching team by bringing in former England assistant Steve Holland, along with Jonathan Woodgate, Jonny Evans, and Travis Binnion.
