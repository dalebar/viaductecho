Adswood Young People's Centre is currently closed due to safety concerns. Stockport Council and Autisk, the charity using the space, are figuring out the next steps. They're seeking a temporary location for Autisk to ensure support continues for families and children. A joint statement emphasised the priority of safety for everyone involved and acknowledged the disappointment this closure brings.
