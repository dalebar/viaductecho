There are planned motorway closures happening in Greater Manchester over the next few days, particularly affecting the M60, M6, and M62. From Monday night (February 16), National Highways has scheduled closures each night. Key closures include:

- M6 Southbound: Multiple entries and exits shut from 21:00 to 06:00
- M61: Few slip road closures from 21:00 to 05:00
- M62: Various closures, especially around Junctions 11 and 20, from 21:00 to 06:00
- M66 and M67 also have several closures from 20:00 to 06:00

Drivers should plan ahead and be mindful of these timings to avoid delays. Stay safe out there!
