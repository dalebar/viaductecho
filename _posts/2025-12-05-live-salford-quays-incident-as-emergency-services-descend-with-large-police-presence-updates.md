A significant emergency services presence is at Salford Quays after a body was discovered in the water near the Salford Watersports Centre. Multiple Greater Manchester Police vehicles, fire engines, an ambulance, and a rapid response vehicle have been dispatched to the scene. Although it's confirmed that a body has been recovered, formal identification has yet to occur. The details surrounding the incident remain unclear, and further updates are anticipated. For anyone feeling overwhelmed, support from Samaritans is available around the clock at 116 123.
