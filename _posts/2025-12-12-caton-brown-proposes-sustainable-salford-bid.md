Mason Caton-Brown, the ex-Salford Red Devils winger, is keen on taking over the liquidated club, focusing on sustainable management. Meanwhile, former chief executive Chris Irwin is teaming up with his own group to rescue the club. The Rugby Football League will announce which bid wins on 17 December.
