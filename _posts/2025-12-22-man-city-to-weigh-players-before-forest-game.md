Pep Guardiola plans to weigh his Manchester City players when they return to training ahead of the match against Nottingham Forest on 27 December, ensuring they're fit and ready. Known for his strict approach to fitness, Guardiola has previously sent players away from first-team training until they hit specific weight targets.
