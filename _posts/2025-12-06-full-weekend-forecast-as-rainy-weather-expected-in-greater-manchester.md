This weekend's looking pretty soggy in Greater Manchester! The Met Office is forecasting rain for both Saturday and Sunday. Today (Saturday), expect a mix of light and heavy showers throughout the day, with temps hitting around 11°C. The rain should ease up around 10pm. Moving into Sunday, it’ll start off dry and cloudy, but more rain’s on the way in the afternoon, possibly heavy, and it’ll be a touch warmer, with highs of about 14°C. Monday's forecast does bring some cheer, with brighter and sunnier spells expected. Overall, it’s going to be a wet one, especially with gusty winds and cloudy skies dominating the North West. Stay dry!
