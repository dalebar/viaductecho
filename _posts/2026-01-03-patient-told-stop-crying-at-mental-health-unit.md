A private mental health unit has been told to improve after inspectors found concerning issues. Patients were given out-of-date medication, and some prescribed drugs were unavailable for over a week. On the Harben ward, which caters to women needing high dependency rehabilitation, residents expressed feelings of unsafety and highlighted that staff appeared too busy to engage with them. The situation has raised serious concerns about patient care and safety.
