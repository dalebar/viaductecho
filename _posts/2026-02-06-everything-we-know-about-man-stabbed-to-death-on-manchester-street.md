A tragic incident unfolded in Northern Moor, Manchester this morning, where a man in his 20s was killed in a suspected stabbing. The police quickly set up a major scene on Southwick Road, with forensic teams working to gather evidence. A 19-year-old suspect has been arrested and is in custody for questioning. Witnesses reported seeing a large emergency response at the crime scene early Friday, with locals expressing shock at the tragic event. The area remains cordoned off as investigations continue, and anyone with information is urged to contact the police or Crimestoppers.
