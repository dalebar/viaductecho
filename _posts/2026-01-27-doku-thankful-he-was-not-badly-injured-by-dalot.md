Manchester City winger Jeremy Doku expressed his relief after suffering no serious injuries from a studs-up challenge by Diogo Dalot during the Manchester derby. Dalot, the Portuguese full-back, shared his thoughts on BBC Radio 5 Live, agreeing it was the right call not to send him off and feeling "really comfortable" with the situation. Doku also faced a late challenge from Wolves defender Yerson Mosquera in City's recent 2-0 victory over Wolves.
