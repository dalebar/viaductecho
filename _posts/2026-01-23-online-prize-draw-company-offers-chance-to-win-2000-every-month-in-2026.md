High Peak Comps is running some cracking prize draws! You’ve got a chance to win £2,000 every month for 2026 for just 35p a ticket, with entries closing on 28 January. They're also giving away hundreds of Lego sets valued at £50,000, with tickets at 99p each, ending on 31 January. If you don’t fancy the Lego, cash alternatives are available.

Recently, they’ve generously supported local causes, donating raffle prizes to charities like Stockport NHS and helping with community projects. High Peak Comps started in 2020 and is all about fair chances to win amazing prizes, having given away an impressive £12 million so far. Plus, they've got a new team member, Lewis, to bring some fun to the mix. For a chance at some exciting prizes, check out their Facebook page and enter the draws!
