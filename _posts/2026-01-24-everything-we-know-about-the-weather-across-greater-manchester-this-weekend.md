This weekend in Greater Manchester kicks off with a dry and bright Saturday, with temperatures hovering around 7C to 8C. However, rain will sweep in by the evening, hitting around 8pm and sticking around into Sunday morning. Expect prolonged rainfall throughout Sunday, with temperatures staying similar to Saturday. By Monday, temperatures will drop a bit to around 3C to 4C, but the start of the week should stay mostly dry. Enjoy the mild weekend while it lasts!
