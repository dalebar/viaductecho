This Saturday, February 21, Greater Manchester is kicking off dry but brace yourself for rain later in the day. A wet spell is expected to roll in after 2pm, bringing around 12 hours of rain until early Sunday morning. Thankfully, it’ll be a bit milder, with daytime temps between 11C and 12C. Sunday starts dry but keep your umbrella handy, as some afternoon and evening showers could pop up. Temperatures will hang around the same as Saturday’s. For the start of next week, expect more rain on Monday, with similar double-digit highs of 10C to 11C.
