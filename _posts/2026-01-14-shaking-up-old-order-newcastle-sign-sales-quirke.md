Newcastle Red Bulls have signed Sale Sharks scrum-half Raffi Quirke on a two-year deal for next season. Quirke, eager to contribute to the club's new chapter, expressed excitement about bringing his energy and attacking style to the team. As the first player linked with the senior England squad to join, his signing marks a significant achievement for the ambitious Red Bulls, aiming to shake up rugby in the north east.
