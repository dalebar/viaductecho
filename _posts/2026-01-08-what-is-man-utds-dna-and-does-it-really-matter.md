Manchester United’s hunt for a new manager post-Ruben Amorim’s sacking has reignited the debate around finding someone who embodies the club's "DNA". Fans often see this as essential for success, promoting a style and spirit intimately linked to the club's identity. The idea isn’t new and echoes through many Premier League clubs that also cling to a notion of "DNA", although it often comes off as overly sentimental.
