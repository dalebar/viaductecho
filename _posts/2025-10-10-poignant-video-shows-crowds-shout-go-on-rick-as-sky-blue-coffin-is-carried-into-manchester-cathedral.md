Fans gathered outside to pay their respects as the boxing legend's coffin was carried in amidst applause. Following his tragic death at 46, thousands lined the streets to bid farewell to the Mancunian icon. The funeral procession, spanning over 15 miles from Tameside to Manchester city centre, made significant stops at locations tied to Ricky's life, including local pubs and gyms.
