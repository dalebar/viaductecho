Macclesfield’s stunning FA Cup victory over holders Crystal Palace had everyone buzzing, with fans flooding the pitch to celebrate and lift captain Paul Dawson. This win was particularly poignant for the Silkmen as they’ve faced tough times, especially after the recent tragic loss of forward Ethan McLeod in a car accident. It’s a reminder of how the FA Cup is often full of surprises and emotional highs, with this upset sure to be remembered alongside other iconic moments, like Crystal Palace’s own FA Cup triumph over Manchester City last May.
