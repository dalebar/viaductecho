Manchester City have nabbed Ghana international Antoine Semenyo from Bournemouth for around £65m. They've outdone local rivals Manchester United, as well as Liverpool, Spurs, and Chelsea, to secure his signature. Semenyo's been a standout performer for Bournemouth this season, and given the current state of transfer fees, City's gotten themselves a decent deal. His skills should definitely boost the team.
