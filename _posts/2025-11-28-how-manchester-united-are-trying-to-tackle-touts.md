Manchester United's facing a right issue with ticket touts, as highlighted in talks with club staff. Their policy states that any ticket downloaded over four times could be blocked at the turnstile. A source from the club mentioned that while they understand these checks might frustrate some fans, many supporters want more measures to ensure away tickets end up with the right people.
