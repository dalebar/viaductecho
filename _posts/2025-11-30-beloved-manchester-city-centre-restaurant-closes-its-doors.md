Platzki, the well-loved restaurant on Deansgate Mews that specialised in modern Polish cuisine, is closing its doors on December 14th due to rising financial pressures. Founded in 2018, it became known for its traditional dishes with a modern twist. The owners shared their heartfelt message on Facebook about the tough decision, citing challenges like high operational costs and rent as reasons for the closure. They expressed deep gratitude for the support they received from their loyal customers, stating they poured their hearts into creating a warm and vibrant community spot. Many locals took to social media to express their sadness and best wishes for the future, recalling the great food and welcoming atmosphere it provided.
