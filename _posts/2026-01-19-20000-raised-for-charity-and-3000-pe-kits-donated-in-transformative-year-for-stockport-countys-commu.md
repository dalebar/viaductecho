Stockport County Community Trust has had a fantastic year, making a big difference in the borough. Their annual impact report highlights key achievements, like donating 3,000 PE kits to local schools and raising £20,000 for charity through matchday events. Over 10,000 residents got involved in various initiatives, including wellbeing groups, walking football, and educational programmes. CEO Alison Warwood shared that they’ve expanded their reach and aim to create lasting change through the power of football. Stockport County CEO Simon Wilson echoed her sentiments, stressing the importance of community unity and the Trust’s role in tackling inequalities. They’re committed to continuing their positive work in the coming year.
