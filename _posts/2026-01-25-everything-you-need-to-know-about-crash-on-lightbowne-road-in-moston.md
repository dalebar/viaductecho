Emergency services responded to a collision on Lightbowne Road in Moston just after midnight on Sunday, January 25. A vehicle hit two parked cars and ended up on its side. A man in his 50s was arrested on suspicion of drink driving. One male was taken to hospital for precautionary checks. Fire crews and a technical response unit arrived promptly to make the area safe, spending about an hour on the scene.
