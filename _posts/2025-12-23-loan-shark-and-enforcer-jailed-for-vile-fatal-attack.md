A loan shark named Marshall and her enforcer have been sentenced to prison for the brutal assault of a vulnerable drug user, Mr Shaw, which ultimately led to his death. The court revealed that Marshall preyed on individuals struggling with addiction. After the attack, she was arrested two days later, claiming she had simply given Mr Shaw a lift home and heard a commotion in the back seat.
