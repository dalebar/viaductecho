Bodycam footage has captured the bravery of PC Spence, who successfully disarmed and detained a suspected armed robber threatening his life. Greater Manchester Police praised the officer for his professionalism and commitment to public safety during a highly dangerous situation. The suspect, who confronted PC Spence multiple times with a weapon and made threats, is currently in custody in a hospital under the Mental Health Act.
