Manchester City are making waves in the Premier League title race by ramping up their squad with 14 new signings since January 1, 2025, costing about £414m. Among them is Guehi, set to become the team's new star centre-back, who is expected to jump right into the first team. This bold move shows City's intent to catch up with Arsenal, who will surely be keeping an eye on their rivals as they bolster their lineup.
