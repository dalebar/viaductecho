Northampton Saints, led by George Hendy, delivered a stunning performance to beat Sale Sharks in the Prem. The Sharks, missing head coach Marco Bortolami and struggling for consistency, had a rocky start and couldn't shake off their defensive issues even during the break. Hendy helped score the Saints' fifth try, showcasing the team's attacking prowess. The Saints' starting lineup included Furbank as captain, alongside key players like Smith, Mitchell, and Coles.
