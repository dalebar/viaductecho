Michael Carrick is the frontrunner to take on the role of Manchester United's caretaker head coach, as per insider sources. The former England midfielder had a solid run with United, featuring in 464 matches from 2006 to 2018 after being brought in by Sir Alex Ferguson from Tottenham.
