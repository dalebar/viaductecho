Tragic news from Macclesfield FC as 21-year-old striker Ethan McLeod has died in a car crash on the M1 after a game against Bedford Town. Ethan was driving home when his white Mercedes hit a barrier near Milton Keynes late on December 16. Despite efforts from emergency services and bystanders, he was pronounced dead at the scene. The club has shared a heartfelt tribute, highlighting Ethan's talent, vibrant personality, and the positive impact he had on his teammates. They expressed deep sorrow over the loss and extended sympathies to his family and friends. Northamptonshire Police are investigating the incident and are seeking witnesses or anyone with dash-cam footage. The motorway was closed for several hours following the accident.
