The Metropolitan and Greater Manchester police have announced plans to arrest anyone carrying signs or chanting "globalise the intifada" during protests. They recognise that this phrase, meaning uprising in Arabic, is causing concern within communities, and they intend to respond firmly to its use in future demonstrations.
