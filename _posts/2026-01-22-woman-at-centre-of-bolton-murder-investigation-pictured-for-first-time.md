Gloria De Lazzari, a 45-year-old woman from Bolton, has been named as the victim in a murder investigation after she was found dead in her flat on January 19. Her family and ex-husband paid heartfelt tributes, describing her as a “great lady with a pure soul” who was kind, compassionate, and deeply devoted to family. Gloria’s mother reminisced about their cherished outdoor walks in Italy, expressing that she will be missed “beyond words”. A man named James Morton, 33, has been charged with her murder and is in custody before his court appearance. Detective Inspector Louise Kelly mentioned that the investigation is ongoing and encouraged anyone with information to contact Greater Manchester Police. There’s a dedicated portal for those who may have relevant footage or details to submit.
