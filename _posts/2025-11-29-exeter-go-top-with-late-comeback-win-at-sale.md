Exeter Chiefs topped The Prem after a thrilling second-half comeback against Sale Sharks. Initially, it seemed the Devon side was set for defeat, with Sale dominating in the classic Friday night weather. A key highlight before the break was England flanker Curry, which sparked plenty of discussion. Sale's director of rugby, Alex Sanderson, led his team with a strong lineup, including players like Ford, Warr, and the Curry brothers. In the end, Exeter's resilience paid off, turning the match in their favour.
