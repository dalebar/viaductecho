The family of a woman tragically killed in a car accident on a country lane has expressed their deep sorrow, saying her loss has "left a huge hole in our hearts". They highlighted her passion for tackling inequality and injustice, emphasising her commitment to charity fundraising and volunteering with the Samaritans.
