Police in Bolton are investigating a murder following the death of a woman at a flat in Kempton Gardens. They were called to the scene around 10am on January 19, where paramedics attempted CPR but the woman sadly passed away shortly after. A 33-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is in custody for questioning. Forensics teams are currently examining the property, which is cordoned off with officers present. Greater Manchester Police are prioritising the identification of the victim to inform her family, with support available for them throughout the investigation.
