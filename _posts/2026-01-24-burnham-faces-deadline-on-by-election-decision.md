Andy Burnham, the Greater Manchester Mayor, has until 5 PM GMT to announce his decision about running in the Gorton and Denton by-election. As a former Labour minister, he hasn't yet confirmed his intentions. Additionally, the NEC might push for an all-women shortlist for potential Labour candidates due to the current male majority among the party's MPs.
