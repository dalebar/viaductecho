Drivers on the M60 near Stockport faced delays this afternoon due to a crash that closed two lanes on the anticlockwise carriageway between junctions one and two. As traffic started to build up, only one lane was open, creating long queues. However, traffic has since begun to ease, and by 13:27 all lanes have been reopened, though there's still some heavy traffic lingering up to the previous closure.
