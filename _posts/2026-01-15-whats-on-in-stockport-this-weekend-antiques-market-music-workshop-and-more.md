Stockport's got a buzzing weekend lined up with loads of fun events! Music lovers can hit up The Spinning Top for free shows featuring Rocket 69, Slackrr, and more on Friday. Saturday brings These Smiths, and Sunday is packed with Dead Highwaymen and others, all free entry! There’s a free community music workshop on Friday for anyone aged 7+ to learn and jam together. On Saturday, don’t miss the antiques fair at Stockport Town Hall to hunt for vintage treasures from 9am-3.30pm. Finally, Disney fans can trade pins at Titan Pops on Sunday from 2-4pm. Sounds like a right laugh, innit? Check out more in the What's On section!
