Police in Droylsden, Tameside, are seeking information after a serious assault on a man in his 30s last night. The incident, which may have involved weapons, occurred around 6pm on Greenside Crescent. The victim suffered serious head injuries and was treated on the scene before being taken to hospital. The area remains cordoned off, with a forensic tent set up for investigation. Detective Inspector Paul Cawsey confirmed the inquiry is in its early stages and urged anyone with CCTV, doorbell, or phone footage to come forward. Local officers are present to reassure the community as they gather more information. Anyone with details is encouraged to contact police via 101 or Crimestoppers at 0800 555 111, referencing log 2715-30/01/2026.
