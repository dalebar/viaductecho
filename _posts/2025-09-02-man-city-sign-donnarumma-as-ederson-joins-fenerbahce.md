Manchester City have welcomed Gianluigi Donnarumma from Paris St-Germain, following the departure of Ederson to Fenerbahce. Ederson, who joined from Benfica in 2017 for £35m, featured in 372 matches for City and was pivotal in their successes, including two FA Cup wins and the Treble in 2022-23. He’s definitely set a high bar as one of City's finest goalkeepers as he bids farewell.
