Anam Rafay, a woman in her 30s, has been identified as the victim in a murder investigation in Wythenshawe after emergency services found her unresponsive at a property on Lulworth Gardens. Believed to have been stabbed, she died at the scene. A 44-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is currently in police custody. Neighbours described the street as quiet, with many knowing each other to some extent, and referred to Ms Rafay as a 'lovely person.' A witness recounted how she banged on a nearby door for help before collapsing.
