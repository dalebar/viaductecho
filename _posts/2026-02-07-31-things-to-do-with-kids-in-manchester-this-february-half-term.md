February half term in Greater Manchester is brimming with exciting activities! The Super Duper Family Festival kicks off with free events from February 18 to 21 at iconic spots like Aviva Studios and Manchester Central Library, featuring crafts, sports, and storytelling. Don't miss the Chinese New Year celebrations, including a spectacular dragon parade in Chinatown on February 15 and creative workshops at the Manchester Art Gallery.

For a unique experience, check out the Horrible Science exhibition at the Science and Industry Museum, launching February 18, and the immersive Bubble Planet experience at Depot Mayfield starting February 7. Families can also hop on themed adventures on the East Lancashire Railway, explore the Tiger Takeover at the safari park, or enjoy a magical forest trail inspired by *Room on the Broom*.

For indoor fun, the National Football Museum and Imperial War Museum offer engaging activities, while kids can build with LEGO at Legoland Discovery Centre.
