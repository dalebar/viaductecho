Ethan McLeod, a forward for Macclesfield, tragically passed away in a car accident on the M1 while returning home from a match on Tuesday. McLeod had a notable history, spending a decade at Wolverhampton Wanderers' academy during his youth. Macclesfield remembered him for his “lust for life” and “unwavering work ethic,” highlighting how he inspired those around him.
