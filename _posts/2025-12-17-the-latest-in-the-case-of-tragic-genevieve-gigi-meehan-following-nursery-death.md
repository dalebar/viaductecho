A coroner is reviewing the nursery culture after the tragic death of nine-month-old Genevieve 'Gigi' Meehan at Tiny Toes Day Nursery in Cheadle Hulme in May 2022. Staff member Kate Roughley swaddled and left Gigi on a bean bag for over an hour, leading to her manslaughter conviction and a 14-year sentence. Another former worker, Rebecca Gregory, was also found guilty of child abuse and received a 3.5-year jail term.
