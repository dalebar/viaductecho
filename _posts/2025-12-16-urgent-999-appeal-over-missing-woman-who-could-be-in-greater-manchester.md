Police are urgently seeking help to locate Laura Hurst, a 30-year-old woman missing from Prenton, Merseyside. She was last seen on CCTV boarding a train in Liverpool and got off at Bryn station around 10:55 PM on December 9. There's a belief she may be in the Ashton-in-Makerfield area now. Merseyside Police have shared her details, noting she's 5ft 7in tall, of medium build, and has red shoulder-length hair. If anyone spots her, they're urged to call 999 immediately.
