Harry Styles fans are eagerly awaiting news about their £20 tickets for his Manchester gig. They had to request tickets through Ticketmaster, with results expected by Tuesday 10 February. Some lucky fans have already confirmed their tickets and are sharing their excitement on TikTok. However, many are still waiting for updates, with some struggling to access the Ticketmaster site due to crashes.
