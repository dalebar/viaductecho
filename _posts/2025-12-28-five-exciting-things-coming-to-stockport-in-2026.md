Stockport is set for a cracking 2026 with several exciting developments on the way. The Weir Mill project will revamp a historic mill overlooking the Mersey, adding 253 homes, new eateries, and public spaces, with completion now pushed to Spring 2026. Over at Merseyway shopping centre, new retailers are on the horizon as foot traffic continues to surge, following a strong year highlighted by the success of Stockroom, a library and events hub. 

Meanwhile, the Stopford Park housing development is introducing 442 new homes, with phase one expected by March 2026. Not to forget the Marple Leisure Hub, which will feature a swimming pool, gym, and library, set to open in the same year. Finally, a new fire station, Stockport Central Community Fire Station, is on track for an Autumn 2026 launch, ensuring local firefighting services remain top-notch. Exciting times ahead for Stockport!
