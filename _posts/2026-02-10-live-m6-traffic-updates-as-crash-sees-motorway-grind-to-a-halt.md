The M6 is facing major delays after a crash blocked three lanes this afternoon between junctions 20 and 21. The incident happened just after 4pm, leading to heavy traffic on the Thelwall Viaduct, with queues building up. Currently, only one lane is clear, and drivers could experience delays of around 20 minutes. Stay tuned for updates on this developing situation!
