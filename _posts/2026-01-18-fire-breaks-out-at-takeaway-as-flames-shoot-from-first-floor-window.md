A takeaway in Little Hulton, Salford, caught fire around 7.30pm, with flames visible from a first-floor window. Four fire crews rushed to the scene on Manchester Road West and managed to keep everyone safe, with no injuries reported. Firefighters from Bolton Central, Farnworth, and Atherton stations are still working to put out the blaze and secure the area. Dramatic photos show their efforts as they tackle the flames in the row of terraced shops.
