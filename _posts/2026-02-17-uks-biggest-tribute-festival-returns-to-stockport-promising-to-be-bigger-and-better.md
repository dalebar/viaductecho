Rockstock Festival is back this May at Woodbank Park, showcasing over 30 live tribute acts to legendary bands. Adult tickets are £16, while kids can enjoy the show for just £10. The festival promises a fantastic atmosphere, with top-notch musicianship and a family-friendly vibe—perfect for budding rockers. It’s a wallet-friendly alternative to bigger festivals like Bloodstock and Download, making it a great choice for music fans of all ages. 

The lineup is impressive, featuring tributes to Metallica, Green Day, Coldplay, and many more across the two-day event. Saturday highlights include acts like Metal Militia and One Eighty Two, while Sunday will see performances from Ultimate Coldplay and Arctic Monkeys, among others. 

For a memorable festival experience without breaking the bank, Rockstock is definitely one to check out! Grab your tickets and get ready for a brilliant weekend of music.
