A teenager, 19, has been arrested on suspicion of murder after a man in his 20s was fatally stabbed on Southwick Road, Northern Moor, Wythenshawe, late on Thursday night. Police were called around 11.35pm to reports of a seriously injured man, who tragically died at the scene. Forensic teams are currently investigating the area, with tents set up and vehicles being examined. Local residents expressed their shock and concern, noting that such incidents are rare in the area. Witnesses are being urged to come forward with any information. Greater Manchester Police is continuing its investigation, and anyone with details can contact them or Crimestoppers anonymously.
