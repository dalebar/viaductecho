A 46-year-old man was tragically found dead at a property on Standfield Drive in Boothstown, Salford, on Saturday morning, December 20. Emergency services, including an air ambulance, responded to the scene around 11.20am. Greater Manchester Police have stated that there are no suspicious circumstances surrounding the death, and the man's family has been informed. GMP expressed their condolences to the family during this difficult time and confirmed that a file has been prepared for the coroner.
