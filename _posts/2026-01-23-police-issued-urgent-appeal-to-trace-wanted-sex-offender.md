Police are on the lookout for Curtis Burke, a 43-year-old wanted sex offender from Stockport. He's wanted for breaching the sex offenders' register and is being recalled to prison. Burke has links to east Manchester, Oldham, Rochdale, and even North Wales. An image of him was shared on social media today, along with a call for public assistance in locating him. If you see him or have any info, get in touch with the investigating officer at 13852@gmp.police.uk or contact Crimestoppers anonymously at 0800 555 111.
