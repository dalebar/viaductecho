Vincent Kompany has paid tribute to boxing legend Ricky Hatton, a cherished Manchester City fan, who tragically passed away at 46. Hatton, who hailed from Stockport, captivated fans with his boxing prowess, winning world titles in two weight classes during the 2000s. His unexpected death sparked an outpouring of grief, leading to heartfelt tributes from sports figures and fans alike, including flowers and tributes at his home. With City playing a derby game against Manchester United shortly after the news broke, a minute's applause was held, signifying his impact in the community.
