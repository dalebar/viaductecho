Stockport's got loads going on this February half term to keep the kids entertained without breaking the bank. There are free workshops, cultural activities, and outdoor fun in the parks. Stockroom is buzzing with family-friendly events like LEGO Club, Children’s Storytime, and a Youth Night for older teens. Don’t miss the Dungeon Crawlers roleplay for young adventurers and the stunning AMAZE projection showcasing local stories. 

Stockport Libraries is also getting in on the action with Crafty Kids Pop-Up events. Plus, on Feb 14, head to the covered mall for Valentine-themed fun with heart-shaped sunglasses and photo ops. There are plenty of inclusive activities for everyone, making it easy for families to enjoy the break together. Check out the Stockroom website or their social media for all the details and booking info. Get out there and make some memories!
