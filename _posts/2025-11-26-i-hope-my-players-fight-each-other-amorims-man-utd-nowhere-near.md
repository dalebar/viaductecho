Everton's recent trip to Old Trafford sparked Ruben Amorim's famous 'the storm is coming' comment, despite Manchester United winning 4-0. For a solid 77 minutes, they faced 10 men at home and still managed to lose, which is a kicker. Amorim even backed David Moyes’ view that the heated exchange between Gueye and Keane reflected a desired level of passion, something he wishes to see from his own side—minus the red card, of course.
