Sunday's win for Manchester United against Arsenal was a stunning display, showcasing a transformed team compared to the end of Amorim's tenure. They needed to excel both offensively and defensively to succeed at the Emirates. Although Arsenal started strong and took an early lead, this seemed to ignite United's performance. Despite Arsenal's mistakes during the match, they've only lost two points in their last three games and still sit four points clear at the top of the table, a position they would have gladly accepted at the season's outset.
