Pep Guardiola, with his cheeky style, expressed he’s "a little bit sad and upset" that Manchester City ranks just seventh in net transfer spending in the Premier League over the last five years. He pointed out that despite City’s past success linked to hefty spending, six other teams now also have to compete for the Premier League, Champions League, and FA Cups due to their higher spending. He emphasised that’s not just his opinion, but a fact, noting Manchester United's whopping £675m spend.
