Sale director of rugby Alex Sanderson has dismissed Argentina head coach Felipe Contepomi's claim that England flanker Tom Curry is a "bully" as "laughable." Sanderson noted that the players who returned from international duty were in great shape and feeling motivated after a successful autumn, securing wins against Australia, Fiji, New Zealand, and Argentina.
