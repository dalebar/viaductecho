Residents of Offerton and Werneth are being warned about 'bogus' gas workers after two burglary incidents in the area. Greater Manchester Police (GMP) highlighted the importance of checking identity cards for anyone visiting your home. They've urged residents to remain cautious, advising not to let anyone in if you're unsure about their credentials. Stay safe and vigilant!
