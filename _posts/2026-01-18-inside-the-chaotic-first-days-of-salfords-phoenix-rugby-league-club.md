It’s a busy Wednesday afternoon at the CorpAcq Stadium in Salford as a new rugby team gears up to debut against Oldham on January 16. After the liquidation of the Salford Red Devils, former captain Ryan Brierley has stepped in as CEO of the phoenix club, navigating a chaotic environment filled with unpacked boxes and last-minute preparations. Ryan reflects on the heartbreak of the Red Devils’ downfall, stressing the importance of the fans who have rallied to support the new club. The local community views the club as more than just a sport; it’s a vital part of their lives. With financial struggles widely known, Ryan emphasizes the need for fiscal discipline to avoid the mistakes of the past, stating, “Don’t spend more than we earn.” He acknowledges the support from Salford council but insists on giving back to the community to ensure the club's sustainable future. As the new era begins, Ryan is both humbled and determined to lead the team forward, honouring the legacy of 152 years of heritage.
