An arson investigation is underway after a bin fire outside a house on Bardsley Street in Lees, Oldham, led to residents needing hospital treatment for smoke inhalation. Emergency services were alerted just before 6:30 am on February 19, with firefighters taking about three hours to extinguish the flames. A man in his 50s has been arrested on suspicion of arson with intent to endanger life and is currently in custody. Detective Inspector Andy Barrett stressed the seriousness of such incidents, highlighting the potential danger involved. Witnesses are encouraged to come forward to assist the investigation, and anyone with information can contact Greater Manchester Police or Crimestoppers anonymously.
