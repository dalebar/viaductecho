Manchester United's head coach, Ruben Amorim, has kept quiet on his transfer budget comments, creating some buzz about possible tensions within the club. He stood firm, saying he doesn't regret his earlier remarks before the match against Newcastle. When pressed further by a journalist about any developments regarding the transfer budget, he again chose not to elaborate, simply stating, "I don't want to talk about that." There's definitely something brewing behind the scenes!
