The sleeper pods are a lifesaver, providing warmth and security for those in need, especially during cold spells like the recent -7C weather. Residents appreciate the welcoming atmosphere, which helps them feel less isolated. Emma, who previously struggled with mental health issues while renting from a housing association for a decade, finds hope and comfort in these new accommodations. The pods come with 24-hour security, and Bolton Council is also providing vital support services, including mental health care and counselling. These pods will remain until March 2026, when their effectiveness will be assessed.
