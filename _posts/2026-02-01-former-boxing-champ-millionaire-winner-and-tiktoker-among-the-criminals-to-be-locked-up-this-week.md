John Eric Spiby, an 80-year-old millionaire lottery winner, was sentenced to 16 years and 6 months for running a sophisticated drugs lab in Wigan, producing fake prescription pills that caused significant harm. His son and an associate also received lengthy sentences for their roles in the conspiracy.
