Police are urgently seeking help to find 14-year-old Grace McCrann, who went missing on Monday afternoon in Merseyside. She was last seen on Grove Road around 5pm and is known to frequent Manchester city centre and Oldham, as well as Liverpool. Grace is described as a white female, roughly 5ft 7in tall, slim, with jet-black hair usually tied back. At the time she went missing, she was wearing black baggy pants, a black top, a 3/4 coat with a fur hood, and white Balenciaga trainers. Authorities are urging anyone who spots her to call 999 for her safety. Additionally, Merseyside Police have requested any other sightings or information to be reported via their website or by calling 101.
