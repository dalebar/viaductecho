Michael Page, a man in his 40s, tragically lost his life in a New Year's Eve car crash at a roundabout in Irlam, Salford. The incident occurred on Cadishead Way, involving one vehicle that overturned after hitting a tree. A woman in her 50s was left seriously injured but her condition hasn't been updated. Friends and family have been sharing tributes on Facebook, expressing their sorrow over Page's untimely passing. Witnesses reported the crash was quite severe, with one man trying to help the victims from the wreckage, describing the scene as harrowing. A cordon remains in place as investigations are ongoing, with police calling it one of the worst collisions they've encountered.
