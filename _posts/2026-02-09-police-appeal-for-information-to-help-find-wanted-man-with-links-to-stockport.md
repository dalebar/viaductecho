Greater Manchester Police are seeking help to locate 34-year-old Calum Brennan, who’s wanted in connection with a burglary in Denton. He is thought to have connections to Stockport and areas like Tameside, Gorton, and Manchester. If you’ve got any info, please reach out to GMP on 0161 856 9259, or you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously at 0800 555 111.
