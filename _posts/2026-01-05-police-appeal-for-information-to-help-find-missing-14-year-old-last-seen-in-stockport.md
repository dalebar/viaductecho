Police in Stockport are looking for 14-year-old Lily, who hasn't been seen since about 5.15pm on Saturday, 3 January 2026. They're increasingly worried for her safety. Lily is a white female, around 5ft 5in tall, with long straight purple/red hair. At the time she went missing, she was wearing a black Hoodrich puffer coat with a fur trim, a black top, grey Nike yoga-style pants, and pink and white Nike trainers. If you have any info that could help, please call 101 or report online through the GMP website, quoting log number 2333 from 03/01/2026.
