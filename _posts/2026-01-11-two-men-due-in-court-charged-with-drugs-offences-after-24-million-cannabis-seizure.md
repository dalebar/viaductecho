Two men, Musa Kala (35) and Raheem Latif (27), have been charged with conspiracy to supply cannabis after police seized around £24 million worth of the drug from a property in Blackrod, Bolton, on January 9. Officers discovered two tonnes of cannabis at Leigh Tenement Farm during a raid. The duo, both from Bolton, have been remanded in custody and are set to appear at Manchester City Magistrates Court on January 12.
