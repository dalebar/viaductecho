Some facilities at Stockport Interchange are closed today due to strike action by Transport for Greater Manchester staff. Ticket and information offices are shut, but bus services are running normally. TfGM apologised for any inconvenience and encouraged people to reach out to their Customer Contact Centre at 0161 244 1000 for queries.
