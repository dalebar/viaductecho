This weekend in Stockport is packed with fun events, supported by local sponsors. At The Spinning Top, enjoy live music featuring the Cassette Apes and Bazooms on Friday, Free 2 Be Bad on Saturday, and a free punk music session on Sunday. Meanwhile, the EatGoodWest Asian food night market takes place on Friday evening at St Mary's Church, offering a variety of delicious Asian dishes along with live music. Don’t miss the vinyl record fair at the Stockport Masonic Guildhall on Sunday, perfect for collectors, running from 10am-4pm. Lastly, there's a talk on renowned Scottish artist Jack Vettriano at the Stockport War Memorial Art Gallery on Sunday afternoon. It’s a great time to get out and about, so check out what’s on in Stockport!
