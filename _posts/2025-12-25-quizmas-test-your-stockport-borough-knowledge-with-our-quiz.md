Get ready for some festive fun with a Stockport-themed quiz! Test your knowledge about the borough through a mix of local history, trivia, and clever clues. Can you name the year Stockport left Cheshire? Or recall which artists didn't record at Strawberry Studios? Don’t forget the cheeky cryptic clues and sequences tied to Stockport County. With ten questions to tackle, it’s the perfect challenge for family and friends this Christmas! Answers provided at the end, so no cheating, eh?
