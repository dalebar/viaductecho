Some facilities at Stockport Interchange are shut today due to strike action from Transport for Greater Manchester staff. Specifically, information and ticket offices are closed, but bus services are still running as usual. TfGM has apologised for the disruption and advised anyone with queries to reach out to their Customer Contact Centre.
