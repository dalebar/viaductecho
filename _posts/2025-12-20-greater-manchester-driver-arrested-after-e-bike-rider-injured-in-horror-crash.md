A driver from Wigan is in custody after a crash on Pimbo Road, Skelmersdale, left an e-biker in his 20s with a serious head injury. The collision involved a black Honda Civic and happened around 2:24 PM on December 19. The rider was quickly taken to hospital, while a 41-year-old man was arrested for suspected drug driving and careless driving causing serious injury. Police have reopened the road after conducting investigations and are seeking witnesses, particularly anyone with dashcam or CCTV footage. They urge those with information to contact them using the provided details. Thanks were extended to the public for their patience during the disruption caused by the road closure.
