Greater Manchester Police are seeking help in locating a missing 14-year-old, Lily, last seen on Grasmere Road in Gatley at 1.50am on Wednesday, December 17. They've expressed growing concern for her wellbeing. Lily is a white female, 5ft 5in tall, with long, straight red/purple hair. At the time she went missing, she wore patterned black pants with side piping, a black top, and a short black hooded winter coat. If you have any information, please get in touch.
