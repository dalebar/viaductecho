A woman named Ms Busby was taken by surprise when her boyfriend, Mr Gavan, proposed under the giant Father Christmas at Manchester's Christmas Markets. She thought they were just posing for a photo, and her shocked reaction has gone viral on social media. She described the proposal as her dream moment, happening in her favourite city during her favourite season. Mr Gavan noticed her astonishment and seemed pleased with the outcome.
