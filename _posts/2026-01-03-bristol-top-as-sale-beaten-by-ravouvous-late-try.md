Bristol Bears clinched a narrow victory over Sale Sharks at Ashton Gate, thanks to Kalaveti Ravouvou's late try, propelling them to the top of The Prem. The Sharks struck first with a try from O'Flaherty after a scramble in the play. Bristol responded well, especially while Rekeiti Ma'asi-White was in the sin-bin, keeping the momentum alive for Jimmy Williams to make a crucial break. Director of rugby Pat Lam shared insights post-match. The team's lineup featured notable players like Rees-Zammit and Genge, showcasing their depth and resilience.
