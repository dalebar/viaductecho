Oldham Road in Manchester has been closed due to a serious crash involving a police car and a black Range Rover. Emergency services, including two fire engines and the Enhanced Rescue Unit, have been on the scene since around 6.05pm. Videos from the incident show significant damage to both vehicles, with the police car appearing to have crashed into railings. The area is currently taped off and a road closure is in place from Thorp Road to Dean Lane. Greater Manchester Police have yet to release further details about the collision, but emergency crews worked together to ensure safety at the scene. Updates will follow as this is still a developing situation.
