A shooting occurred on School House Road in Oldham, leaving multiple bullet holes in a home's front window. Greater Manchester Police were called on Sunday, February 15, after reports of criminal damage. Upon arrival, they discovered that a gunman had opened fire, but fortunately, no injuries were reported. Forensic officers are investigating the scene, and a cordon is in place as police conduct their enquiries. They are looking for witnesses or anyone with CCTV or dash cam footage related to the incident. Police advise anyone with information to contact them quoting log 1397 of 15/02/2026 or report anonymously through Crimestoppers.
