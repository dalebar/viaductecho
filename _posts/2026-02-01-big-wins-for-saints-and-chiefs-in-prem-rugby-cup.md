Northampton Saints bagged the East Midlands bragging rights, thrashing Leicester Tigers 40-22 in the Prem Rugby Cup at Franklin's Gardens. Over in Pool A, Exeter Chiefs cruised to victory against Sale Sharks, racking up an impressive nine tries for a convincing 59-14 win at Sandy Park.
