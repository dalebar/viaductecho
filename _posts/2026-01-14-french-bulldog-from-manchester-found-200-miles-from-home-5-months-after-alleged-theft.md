Tilly, a French bulldog stolen from Manchester, has been found in Ayr, Scotland, five months after her disappearance. The not-for-profit Ayrshire Drone Dog Rescue, along with local police and vets, helped locate her 200 miles away from home. They kept details under wraps to ensure her safety during the search. Now, there's hope for a reunion with her owner this week. Tilly's owner publicly thanked everyone involved, expressing their eagerness to have their beloved pet back. The Ayrshire Drone Dog Rescue, which started in June 2022, focuses on reuniting missing dogs and promoting animal welfare, claiming to have reunited hundreds since it began.
