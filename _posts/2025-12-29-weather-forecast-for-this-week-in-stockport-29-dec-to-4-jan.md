The weather in Stockport this week is set to be mainly partly cloudy with cooler temperatures and low chances of rain. Monday kicks off with highs of 5°C, dropping to 3°C, while Tuesday cools down to 4°C at the peak. Midweek winds will pick up, reaching 16 mph on Wednesday, with temperatures hitting a low of -1°C. Thursday is the warmest day, with a high of 6°C but a chance of rain increases to 39%. Expect temperatures to dip again on Friday to 3°C, while Saturday and Sunday see even cooler conditions, with highs around 0°C and lows below freezing. 

Commuters should bundle up, especially midweek. If you’re planning outdoor activities over the weekend, Saturday looks the best, but do stay warm and flexible with your plans due to the chilly spell. Enjoy the week!
