A 46-year-old man was found dead at a house on Standfield Drive in Boothstown, Salford. Emergency services, including an air ambulance, responded to the scene on Saturday morning at 11.20am. Although multiple ambulances were present, Greater Manchester Police stated there are no suspicious circumstances surrounding the death. A coronial investigation is anticipated, and a police spokesperson expressed condolences to the man's loved ones.
