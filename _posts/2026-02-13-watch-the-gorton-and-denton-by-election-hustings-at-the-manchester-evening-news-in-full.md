The Gorton and Denton by-election is heating up, with candidates taking part in a lively Manchester Evening News hustings. Tensions were evident from the start, especially when Labour’s Angeliki Stogia greeted Reform UK's Matt Goodwin with a pointed remark. Green candidate Hannah Spencer threw her hat in the ring, branding the election a matchup between a true Mancunian and an outsider from the Reform party. Goodwin countered by accusing Labour of neglecting the area and critiqued Green Party drug proposals, stating they’d spell disaster for Gorton and Denton. It's a fierce contest, and you can catch the full hustings online!
