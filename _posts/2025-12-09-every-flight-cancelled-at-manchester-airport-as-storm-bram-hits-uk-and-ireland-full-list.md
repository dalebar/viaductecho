Storm Bram is causing chaos at Manchester Airport with flight cancellations due to fierce winds and heavy rain across the UK and Ireland. Greater Manchester has faced downpours and gusty winds, and the Met Office has put out 10 weather warnings, including four amber alerts for rain and wind in various regions. A yellow alert for heavy rain is also in place for northern areas of Greater Manchester until 3pm. Dublin Airport has seen 42 cancellations as Ireland braces for severe weather. Most cancellations at Manchester are from Aer Lingus to destinations like Dublin and Belfast. Keep an eye out for updates on the affected flights throughout the day.
