High Peak Comps is rolling out some brilliant prize draws soon! You can enter to win a dream safari holiday valued at £15,000 or opt for a £10,000 cash alternative for just 29p per ticket. The competition closes on Sunday 15 February. They're also hosting an instant win contest with a whopping £250,000 prize pot featuring over 10,000 prizes, from £5,000 to smaller amounts, at 89p a ticket. This one wraps up on Sunday 1 March, with every ticket giving you a shot at a £10,000 grand draw too.

Beyond the prizes, High Peak Comps actively supports local causes, recently donating to schools, hospitals, and charities. Established in 2020, they've made a name for themselves, winning awards for their community efforts. If you're keen on local initiatives, they’re open to support as well. For more info, check them out on their Facebook page!
