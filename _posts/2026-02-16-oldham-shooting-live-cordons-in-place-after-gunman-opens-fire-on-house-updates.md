Two police cordons are set up in Oldham following a shooting on School House Road that occurred on Sunday, February 15. Officers responded to reports of criminal damage and discovered firearms had been discharged, but thankfully, there were no injuries. The investigation is ongoing, with forensic teams working at the scene, and police are appealing for any witnesses or anyone with CCTV or dash cam footage to come forward. 

The cordons are in place on both School House Road and nearby Roundthorn Road, where police activity has been visible, including several police vehicles. The Greater Manchester Police are urging people to contact them at 101, quoting log 1397 of 15/02/2026, or to provide information anonymously through Crimestoppers. Updates on the situation will be shared as more information becomes available.
