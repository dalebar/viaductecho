Greater Manchester Police has named Chief Superintendent Natalie Dalby as the new district commander for Stockport, leading the Neighbourhood Crime Team. With 25 years of experience in various roles, including the Sex Offenders' Management Unit, she aims to tackle crime comprehensively. Her goals include reducing neighbourhood crime, enhancing investigation standards, and improving victim support. Dalby plans to build on the successes of the team, which has already reduced burglaries, shoplifting, and personal robbery. She expressed her commitment to ensuring that everyone can live without the threat of crime, noting the team's progress over the past couple of years. Looking ahead to 2026, she is optimistic about continuing to achieve positive outcomes for the community.
