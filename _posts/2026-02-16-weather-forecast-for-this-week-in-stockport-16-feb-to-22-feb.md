Stockport's weather this week is mostly cloudy with some rain expected. Temperatures will be cool, ranging from a low of -0°C on Wednesday to a mild 13°C on Sunday. 

- **Monday**: Start off with clouds and rain, highs of 7°C. Windy at 26mph.
- **Tuesday**: More clouds in the afternoon, temperatures between 0°C and 5°C with less chance of rain.
- **Wednesday**: Overcast with light rain likely, highs of 5°C and brisk winds.
- **Thursday**: Afternoon clearing, rising to 7°C, with a lower chance of rain.
- **Friday**: Ongoing cloud cover, rain expected late, highs of 9°C.
- **Saturday**: Cloudy with easing rain later in the day, highs around 10°C.
- **Sunday**: Cloudy with chances of rain and temperatures reaching 13°C.

Compared to previous weeks, it’ll feel a tad warmer by the weekend, but still quite wet. Commuters should pack their umbrellas, especially on Monday and Friday. For weekend plans, Saturday looks best for outdoor activities, but keep an eye on the weather for Sunday.
