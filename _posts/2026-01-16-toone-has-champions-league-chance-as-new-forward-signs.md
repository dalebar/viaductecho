Manchester United manager Marc Skinner has hinted that Ella Toone might be fit for the Champions League games against Atletico Madrid in February. Before last Saturday, Toone had been a regular in the Women's Super League, netting three goals and assisting five times this season. United have also bolstered their attack with Swedish striker Wangerheim and Germany’s Lea Schuller.
