Søstrene Grene has just opened a new store in Stockport town centre, celebrated with a vibrant ceremony featuring musicians and dancers. Located in the former JD Sports unit at Merseyway Shopping Centre, the store offers a range of affordable home decor, craft gifts, and art supplies. The opening was met with a fantastic turnout, marking it as one of the busiest for Søstrene Grene in the North of England. The brand, founded in Denmark in 1973 and now managed by the founders’ sons, aims to provide a calming shopping experience and showcase its latest concepts. Merseyway Centre Manager Victoria Nichol is thrilled with the new addition, highlighting its modern and unique offerings that promise to delight local shoppers.
