Greater Manchester is home to some stunning dream homes, particularly in Bowdon, Trafford. Stanhope Road is the priciest street, with an average house costing around £3,075,000. Recently, a lavish six-bedroom property was listed at £3,395,000, down from £3,490,000. Spanning 6,800 square feet, this detached home features five reception rooms, six bathrooms, and luxury amenities like an indoor swimming pool, gym, games room, and cinema. The opulent entrance hall leads to a spacious kitchen, family room, and a swimming pool complex. The first floor offers a grand main bedroom suite and additional en-suites for other bedrooms. There's even a games room or potential fifth bedroom and a sixth bedroom with an en-suite on the top floor. Outside, you’ll find ample parking, a gated entrance, and lovely gardens. With high-grade security, it’s marketed as a remarkable family residence that really needs to be seen to appreciate its scale and elegance.
