Gary Neville's calling out Manchester United to stop the tinkering and find a manager that really gets the club's vibe after Ruben Amorim was let go. Meanwhile, Jamie Carragher thinks taking over from Amorim at United would be a tempting role for any gaffer. Amorim made quite a name for himself with his fiery press conferences since joining in November 2024. United's captain Bruno Fernandes even shared some love on social media for Amorim following his sacking.
