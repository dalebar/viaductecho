Flu cases in the UK are spiking, averaging around 2,660 cases daily in hospitals, like filling up three hospitals with patients. Influenza spreads easily through sneezing and coughing. Last winter saw about 8,000 deaths in England, while three winters ago, nearly 16,000 people lost their lives to the virus. Gill Dummigan mentioned that the flu vaccine isn't foolproof, as scientists base their predictions on past winters in the UK and Australia.
