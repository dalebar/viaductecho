This morning at Manchester Airport, an Aer Lingus plane arriving from Barbados was met by fire engines due to a ‘technical issue’. Footage from Think Planes showed emergency crews on the scene as the aircraft landed safely. Passengers remained on the plane during inspections but were eventually allowed to disembark without incident. Aer Lingus confirmed that the response was purely precautionary, with no additional details about the technical issue shared.
