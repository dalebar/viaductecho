A man in a wheelchair, Mr. Dawson, shared his "frustration and anger" after being stranded at a Metrolink tram stop in Greater Manchester. He was out with family and heading to meet a friend when he faced accessibility issues. Mr. Dawson acknowledged that full accessibility is a challenging goal. Meanwhile, a spokesperson for Transport for Greater Manchester mentioned that some Metrolink lifts are out of service due to vandalism and repairs are pending on others.
