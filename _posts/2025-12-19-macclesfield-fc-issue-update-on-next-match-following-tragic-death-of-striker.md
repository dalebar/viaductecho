Macclesfield FC have postponed their upcoming match following the tragic death of player Ethan McLeod, who was just 21. He sadly passed away in a car accident on the M1 after returning from a game. The club announced the postponement of their away fixture against Alfreton Town, expressing gratitude for the understanding shown by Alfreton and the National League. Fans have praised this decision, reflecting on Ethan's talent and character, with many sharing their condolences on social media. In memory of Ethan, Macclesfield FC has opened a book of condolence for supporters, which will be shared with his family. The club stated they are heartbroken and mourning deeply for their talented teammate, who made a significant impact on everyone around him. The book will be available to sign at Bar Twenty Seven during specified hours on non-matchdays.
