A car crashed into the shop front of an estate agent in Prestwich on Monday afternoon, following a collision involving four vehicles. Police and emergency services quickly arrived at Bury Old Road to manage the scene and cordon off part of the pavement. Fortunately, no serious injuries were reported, although one woman was taken to the hospital. The fire service confirmed that nobody was trapped in the vehicles. The accident caused significant traffic disruption as the road was blocked by emergency vehicles.
