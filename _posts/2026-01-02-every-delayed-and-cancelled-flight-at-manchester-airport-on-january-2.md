This weekend, thousands are off on winter getaways or returning to the UK after the holidays. If you're flying soon, keep an eye on your flight schedule as changes can pop up last minute due to staffing, air traffic control, or winter weather. The UK is facing a cold snap, and snow could lead to delays. Manchester Airport is ready to clear snow quickly if it hits, but flights to Amsterdam are already facing cancellations due to weather at Schiphol. 

As of January 2, here are some flight updates: easyJet and KLM had cancelled flights to Amsterdam, while several others have estimated new departure times. If you're heading out, remember to check your airline for the latest info. Safe travels, and stay warm out there!
