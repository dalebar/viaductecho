A large fire broke out Wednesday evening, October 15, on Lonsdale Road in Levenshulme, south Manchester, leading to the evacuation of five nearby homes. Emergency crews arrived around 6:35pm to tackle the blaze, which originated in an outdoor shed and sent significant smoke into the air. Three fire engines from local stations were deployed, and firefighters used breathing apparatus and jets to extinguish the flames. The scene was cordoned off as efforts continued until about 8pm. Greater Manchester Fire and Rescue Service confirmed that residents were evacuated as a precaution while crews worked on the situation. The incident drew attention from local residents, who watched in shock.
