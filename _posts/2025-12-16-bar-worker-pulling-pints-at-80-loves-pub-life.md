An 80-year-old woman shares her love for the pub life, mentioning how much she enjoys serving and caring for people in her job behind the bar. Reflecting on her past, she recalls her younger days of wearing heels, though she humorously notes she can't manage them anymore. She feels deeply connected to the pub culture, which has been a constant in her life.
