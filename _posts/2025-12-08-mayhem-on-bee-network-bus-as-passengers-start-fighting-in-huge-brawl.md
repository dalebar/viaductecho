A man in his 50s got injured in a brawl on the Bee Network bus in Oldham, specifically on the 409 service to Rochdale, on Sunday evening. The scuffle, which happened around 7.30pm, was caught on mobile footage that's now circulating online. The fight broke out on the lower deck of the bus near the driver's seat, with passengers grappling and throwing punches while others watched. Greater Manchester Police were called to the scene, confirming the man sustained minor injuries. No arrests have been made yet, and an investigation is currently in progress.
