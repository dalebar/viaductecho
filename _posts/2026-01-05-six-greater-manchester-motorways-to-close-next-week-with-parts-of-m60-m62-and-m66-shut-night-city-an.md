Next week, six motorways in Greater Manchester will close overnight as folks head back to work, school, and college. The closures, from January 5 to January 8, affect parts of the M60, M602, M61, M62, M66, and M67, mostly starting at 9pm and reopening by 6am. 

Key points for drivers: 

- M60 Anti-clockwise: multiple slip road closures at Jct 1, 5, and 25
- M60 Clockwise: closures between Jct 12 and 13
- M61 Southbound: A666 to A580 link road closure
- M62 link road closures to the M60
- M67 Eastbound: Jct 2 entry slip closure continues until February 2026

Fans attending the Manchester United and Manchester City matches on January 5 should plan accordingly, as many affected roads will be busy. Best to check the specific closures listed and give yourself extra travel time!
