A man has been charged with several offences after allegedly attacking hotel staff in Manchester on January 11. Greater Manchester Police reported that the staff were threatened during the incident, though the hotel's name hasn’t been revealed. Frank Delahaye, a 60-year-old with no fixed address, is facing charges that include possession of a firearm with intent to cause fear and possession of a knife. Additionally, he’s been charged with assault, criminal damage, and two counts of possessing controlled drugs. Delahaye remains in custody and is set to appear at Manchester Crown Court on February 18.
