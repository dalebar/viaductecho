Pep Guardiola has expressed his apologies for a confrontation with a cameraman after Manchester City's Premier League loss to Newcastle on Saturday. He admitted feeling embarrassed about the incident but didn't disclose the details of their exchange. City is set to face Bayer Leverkusen in the Champions League on Tuesday, looking to keep their unbeaten streak going and strengthen their position in the group stage, where they currently sit fourth. A win would help them secure a spot in the knockout rounds.
