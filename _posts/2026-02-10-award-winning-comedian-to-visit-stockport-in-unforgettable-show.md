Award-winning comedian Russell Kane is set to perform in Stockport this year on Sunday, 29 March, at the Plaza, presented by Chuckl comedy club. Known for his high-energy performances and sharp wit, Kane has been featured on shows like Live at the Apollo and Mock the Week. He’ll be supported by a top-notch lineup of stand-up comedians. The show kicks off at 7.30pm, with tickets starting from £28.60. Don't miss out on what promises to be a memorable night!
