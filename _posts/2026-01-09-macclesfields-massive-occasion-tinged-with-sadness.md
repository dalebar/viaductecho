Macclesfield Town is gearing up for a momentous FA Cup third-round clash against the holders, Crystal Palace, this Saturday. It's a massive occasion for the lowest-ranked team left in the tournament, but it's also a bittersweet moment as the club mourns the recent loss of player McLeod, who passed away 25 days ago. McLeod, who started his career with Macclesfield and rejoined in 2023, faced injury challenges but still made an impact, scoring three goals in seven games. The match marks a significant day in Macclesfield's history while the team and fans remember McLeod's contributions.
