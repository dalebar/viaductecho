Greater Manchester has been experiencing a mysterious pink glow in the night skies, particularly over Bolton and Manchester, which many initially thought was the Northern Lights. However, it turns out the glow is from LED lights used to enhance the quality of football pitches at Manchester City’s Ethihad Stadium and Bolton Wanderers’ Toughsheet Community Stadium. Chris Simm, Bolton's head groundsman, explained that these lights are essential for maintaining grass growth, especially since the stadium stands block natural sunlight. The glow is a result of new technology aimed at mimicking sunlight, specifically designed to keep pitches suitable for professional football during the winter months. While clubs have used lighting rigs for years, this latest tech creates the distinct pink hue.
