Police boarded a Manchester-bound train at Macclesfield station after reports of a man with a knife. This happened on December 18, just after 1:44 pm. Officers removed two men from the Avanti West Coast service and searched them, but no weapons were found. After the search, the men were released without further action. British Transport Police encourages passengers to report any suspicious behaviour by texting 61016.
