Stockport has got a jam-packed weekend with tons of exciting events! Kicking off with the EatGoodWest Asian food market at St Mary's on Friday, it’s a festive delight with authentic cuisine, live music, and a cozy vibe inside a historic church. The Underbank Weekender runs from Friday to Sunday, showcasing over 60 artists across various venues, with food and a pop-up bar to boot. If you're in the mood for theatre, catch "Jeeves and Wooster in Perfect Nonsense" at Chads Theatre this Friday and Saturday. The fun continues with "Aladdin" at Stockport Plaza starting Friday and running to January 4. Kids can meet Father Christmas at Staircase House and enjoy crafts on Saturday and Sunday, while Poynton Christmas Market on Sunday brings artisan food and gifts to explore. Wrap up your weekend with a hearty carvery at Waterside Hotel, served all Sunday. There’s something for everyone, so get out and enjoy!
