Two more arrests have been made in connection with a shooting in Urmston, Trafford. The incident took place on Higher Road on January 16, where a 21-year-old man was shot and is now in a stable but critical condition in hospital. Initially, an 18-year-old was arrested on suspicion of attempted murder. Now, a 21-year-old and a 28-year-old have also been taken into custody for the same charges. GMP has stated that the shooting appears to be a targeted attack, with no further threat to the community, and patrols have increased in the area. Detectives are urging anyone with information, particularly CCTV or dash cam footage, to come forward. Senior officers reassured the public of their commitment to safety and justice, emphasising that they are pursuing every lead to apprehend those responsible. For information, please contact GMP quoting log 3132 of 16/01/2026, or report anonymously via Crimestoppers.
