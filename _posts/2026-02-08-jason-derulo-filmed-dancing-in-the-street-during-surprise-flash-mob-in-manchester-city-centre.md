Jason Derulo surprised fans in Manchester with a flash mob performance on New Cathedral Street, dancing to his new single 'Sexy for Me' alongside a large crew of dancers. This spontaneous event took place before his concert at Co-op Live arena on February 7 as part of The Last Dance 2026 tour. Despite the rain, Derulo and his dancers wowed the gathered crowd, which included videographers capturing the moment. Fans went wild on social media, sharing videos and expressing their excitement, with comments like "Omg how did I miss this!" and praise for the performance. Just hours later, Derulo delivered a dynamic two-hour show at the arena, showcasing his talent and iconic hits from earlier in his career.
