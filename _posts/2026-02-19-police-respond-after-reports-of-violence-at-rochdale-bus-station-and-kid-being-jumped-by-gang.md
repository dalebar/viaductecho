Police responded to reports of violence at Rochdale bus station on February 17, where a large group of about 30 youths reportedly attacked a child. Witnesses expressed concern on social media, urging parents to ensure their kids are safe and talking to them. The incident occurred around 5.40pm near the Riverside centre, involving local schoolchildren but thankfully no serious injuries were reported. Greater Manchester Police have confirmed they're investigating but no arrests have been made yet.
