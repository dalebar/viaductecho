A crash occurred today on Manchester Road in Ashton-under-Lyne around 1:45pm, involving a pedestrian. Greater Manchester Police and the North West Ambulance Service responded to the scene, with an air ambulance landing in a nearby park. The area was cordoned off, and multiple emergency vehicles were present. A man was taken to hospital with serious injuries, according to NWAS. Eyewitnesses reported seeing a person on the pavement and others rushing to help.
