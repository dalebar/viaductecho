Mount’s got a special place in my heart, I’ll tell you that! He’s a Champions League and Club World Cup winner with Chelsea, and recently, he was an injury-time sub in United’s 2024 FA Cup final win against Manchester City. He also started in the Europa League final loss to Tottenham and was part of the squad that had a rough finish in 15th place. On the international scene, he’s notched up 36 caps for England, with his latest in that tough quarter-final against France in the 2022 World Cup.
