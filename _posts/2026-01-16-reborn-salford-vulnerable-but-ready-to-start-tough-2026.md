Just six weeks ago, Salford Red Devils faced winding-up by the High Court over hefty debts, officially winding up on 3 December. This came when most Championship teams had already sorted their business for the upcoming season. Former captain Ryan Brierley has moved into the chief executive role, while ex-player Mason Caton-Brown heads up the consortium that took over the club, working hard to prepare for the new season. With players leaving for other clubs, Salford had to rely on younger sides to meet their fixture commitments.
