The £2 cap on single fares in Greater Manchester's transport will stick around for another year. Right now, concessionary passes can only be used after 9:30 am, but Mayor Andy Burnham thinks it’s time to lift that restriction on buses. He mentioned they're still developing the Bee Network, which aims to improve transport across the region. You can catch updates on BBC Radio Manchester and follow them on social media.
