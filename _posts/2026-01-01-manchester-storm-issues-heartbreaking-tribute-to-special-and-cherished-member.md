The Manchester Storm ice-hockey team has paid a heartfelt tribute to Cherry Woodhams, their beloved massage therapist, who recently passed away after a battle with cancer. Based in Altrincham, Cherry played a vital role in supporting players' muscle recovery and preventing injuries. The club described her as trusted, respected, and deeply loved, highlighting her warmth and the positive impact she had on everyone around her. Fans and friends have shared tributes online, recalling her radiant smile and kind personality. The team expressed their grief in a statement, thanking her for her dedication and the lasting mark she left on the Storm family. They extended their thoughts to her family and friends during this difficult time, concluding with a heartfelt rest in peace for Cherry, who will always be a part of the Storm family.
