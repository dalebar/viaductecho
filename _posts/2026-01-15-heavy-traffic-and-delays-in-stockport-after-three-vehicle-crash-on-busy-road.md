A crash involving three vehicles on Stockport Road West in Bredbury, near Morrisons, caused significant traffic delays this morning (January 15). Emergency services responded promptly, and while there were reports of 'minor injuries', the road was blocked, leading to heavy congestion. Locals took to social media to share their experiences of the long queues. By around 8.30pm, Greater Manchester Police reported that the incident was being cleared.
