Police in Bolton are investigating a murder after a woman was found dead in a flat on Kempton Gardens on January 19. A 33-year-old man is in custody as detectives gather more evidence. Initial reports arrived over concerns for her welfare, but despite CPR attempts, she could not be saved. Forensic teams have been combing the scene and a police cordon remains in place, causing concern among local residents. Neighbours expressed shock at the incident, noting the area usually experiences drug-related issues, but nothing like this before. Residents described the heavy police presence and the unsettling nature of a murder happening so close to home. Greater Manchester Police are focused on identifying the victim and supporting her family, assuring the community there is no wider danger. They encourage anyone with information to reach out via the provided contact details.
