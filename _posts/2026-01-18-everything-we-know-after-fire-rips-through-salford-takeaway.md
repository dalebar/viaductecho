A fire erupted at the Route 92 burger bar in Little Hulton, Salford, last night, causing substantial damage. Flames were spotted coming from a first-floor window on Manchester Road West around 7:30pm, sparking a swift response from four fire crews. Dramatic images showed smoke billowing from above the takeaway. Thankfully, no injuries were reported. GMFRS has initiated an investigation into the fire's cause. Crews from Bolton Central, Farnworth, and Atherton worked tirelessly to extinguish the flames and secure the building. The fire primarily impacted the first floor, and firefighters remained on-site to ensure safety.
